HouseCanary

Description

HouseCanary is an AI platform for property valuation and market analytics, providing real estate agents with accurate data, forecasts, and insights for smarter decision-making.

Company & History

Founded in 2013, HouseCanary is a leader in real estate analytics and automated valuation models (AVMs) in the U.S.

Key Features

  • AI-driven property valuation
  • Market analytics and forecasting
  • Customizable reports and dashboards
  • Data integration and export
  • API for enterprise clients

How to Use

  • Request a demo at housecanary.com.
  • Access property data and analytics tools.
  • Generate reports and share insights with clients.

Pricing
