Wysa

Description

Wysa is an AI-powered mental health support app that offers self-care exercises, mood tracking, and access to professional therapists. It uses conversational AI to provide evidence-based support for stress, anxiety, and depression.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, Wysa is used by millions worldwide and is recognized for its privacy-first approach and clinically validated techniques.

Key Features

  • 24/7 AI chatbot for mental health support
  • Guided self-care and mindfulness exercises
  • Mood tracking and journaling
  • Access to human therapists (paid)
  • Personalized mental health plans

How to Use

  • Download the Wysa app from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Start chatting with the AI for instant support and exercises.
  • Upgrade for access to professional therapists and advanced features.

Pricing

  • Free: AI chatbot and basic self-care tools
  • Premium: Access to therapists and advanced features (subscription)
