Canva Magic Design

Description

Canva Magic Design is an AI-powered feature within Canva that generates design suggestions, layouts, and templates based on your content and preferences. It helps users create professional graphics, presentations, and more with minimal effort.

Company & History

Canva, founded in 2012 in Australia, is a global leader in online design tools. Magic Design is part of Canva's suite of AI features, launched to make design accessible to everyone, regardless of skill level.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic Magic Design features and templates.
  • Pro: Access to premium templates, AI features, and brand kits.
  • Teams/Enterprise: Collaboration, admin controls, and advanced integrations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at canva.com.
  • Choose Magic Design from the homepage or editor.
  • Upload your content or describe your project.
  • Pick from AI-generated design suggestions and customize as needed.

Key Features

  • AI-powered design suggestions
  • Automatic layout and template generation
  • Brand kit and style matching
  • Collaboration and sharing tools
  • Integration with Canva's full design suite

Pricing

  • Free: Basic features
  • Pro: $14.99/month (or $119.99/year)
  • Teams/Enterprise: Custom pricing
