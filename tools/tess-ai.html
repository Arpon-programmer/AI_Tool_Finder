Tess AI

Description

Tess AI is an AI-powered psychological support chatbot designed to deliver real-time emotional support, psychoeducation, and self-help interventions via text messaging. It is used by individuals and organizations for scalable mental health care.

Company & History

Tess AI is developed by X2AI, a company specializing in digital mental health solutions since 2014. Tess is deployed in multiple languages and settings worldwide.

Key Features

  • 24/7 AI-driven psychological support
  • Personalized conversations and interventions
  • Integration with SMS, web, and messaging apps
  • Scalable for organizations and healthcare providers
  • Multilingual support

How to Use

  • Access Tess AI via web or through partner organizations.
  • Start a conversation for instant support and self-help exercises.
  • Organizations can integrate Tess AI for employee or patient support.

Pricing

  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for organizations and partners
