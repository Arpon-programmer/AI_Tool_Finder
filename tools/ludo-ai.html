Ludo AI

Description

Ludo AI is an AI-powered platform for game ideation, research, and concept generation, helping developers create innovative game ideas and mechanics.

Company & History

Founded in 2020, Ludo AI is used by game studios and indie developers to accelerate the creative process and discover new trends in gaming.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited access to ideation tools.
  • Pro: Full access and advanced features.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions and analytics.

How to Use

  • Sign up at ludo.ai.
  • Explore trending game ideas and mechanics.
  • Use AI to generate and refine concepts.
  • Export and share your ideas with your team.

Key Features

  • AI-powered game ideation
  • Trend analysis and research
  • Concept generation and refinement
  • Collaboration tools

Pricing

  • Free: Limited access
  • Pro: Subscription pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
