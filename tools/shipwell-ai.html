Shipwell AI

Description

Shipwell AI is a freight management and logistics automation platform that uses artificial intelligence to optimize shipping, track loads, and improve supply chain efficiency.

Company & History

Shipwell is a leading provider of AI-driven freight and logistics solutions, helping shippers and carriers automate operations and improve visibility.

Versions & Plans

  • Enterprise: Solutions for shippers and carriers
  • Custom: Tailored deployments for large organizations

How to Use

  • Contact Shipwell for a demo or integration.
  • Integrate with your TMS, ERP, or carrier systems.
  • Use AI dashboards for freight management and analytics.

Key Features

  • Automated load tracking
  • Predictive analytics for shipping
  • Integration with logistics platforms
