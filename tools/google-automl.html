Google AutoML

Description

Google AutoML is a suite of machine learning products that enables developers with limited ML expertise to train high-quality models for vision, language, and structured data. It automates the process of model selection, training, and tuning.

Company & History

Launched by Google Cloud, AutoML is part of the Vertex AI platform. It is widely used by businesses and researchers to accelerate AI adoption without deep ML knowledge.

Versions & Plans

  • AutoML Vision: For image classification and object detection.
  • AutoML Natural Language: For text classification and entity extraction.
  • AutoML Tables: For structured data and tabular prediction.

How to Use

  • Sign up at Google Cloud AutoML.
  • Upload your data and select a task.
  • Let AutoML train and evaluate models automatically.
  • Deploy models via API or integrate with Google Cloud services.

Key Features

  • Automated model training and tuning
  • Support for vision, language, and tabular data
  • Easy deployment and integration
  • Model evaluation and explainability
  • Scalable on Google Cloud

Pricing

  • Pay-as-you-go pricing based on usage
  • Free tier available for limited usage
