IBM SPSS AI

Description

IBM SPSS AI integrates artificial intelligence and machine learning into the SPSS platform for advanced statistical analysis, predictive modeling, and data mining.

Company & History

SPSS was founded in 1968 and acquired by IBM in 2009. IBM SPSS is a leading analytics platform used by researchers, analysts, and businesses worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Base: Core statistical analysis tools.
  • Standard: Advanced statistics and forecasting.
  • Professional: Data mining and predictive analytics.
  • Premium: Full suite with AI and machine learning features.

How to Use

  • Download or access IBM SPSS from ibm.com.
  • Import your data and select analysis tools.
  • Use AI features for predictive modeling and automation.
  • Export results and reports for decision making.

Key Features

  • Advanced statistical analysis
  • AI-powered predictive modeling
  • Data mining and automation
  • Integration with Python and R
