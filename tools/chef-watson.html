Chef Watson

Description

Chef Watson is an AI-powered creative recipe suggestion tool developed by IBM, designed to inspire chefs with unique ingredient combinations and innovative dishes.

Company & History

Chef Watson was created by IBM as part of its Watson AI platform, showcasing the use of AI in culinary creativity and food science.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Publicly available as a demonstration of AI in cooking.

How to Use

  • Visit IBM Chef Watson (if available).
  • Input ingredients or preferences to generate creative recipes.
  • Explore new flavor pairings and cooking ideas.
