Zillow Premier Agent

Description

Zillow Premier Agent is an AI-powered real estate lead generation and CRM platform, helping agents connect with buyers and sellers, manage leads, and grow their business.

Company & History

Launched by Zillow Group, Premier Agent is a leading solution for real estate professionals in the U.S. market.

Key Features

  • AI-driven lead generation and matching
  • CRM and pipeline management
  • Automated follow-ups and reminders
  • Performance analytics and reporting
  • Integration with Zillow listings

How to Use

  • Sign up at premieragent.zillow.com.
  • Set up your agent profile and preferences.
  • Manage and nurture leads through the platform.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on market and lead volume
