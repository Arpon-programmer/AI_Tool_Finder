Primer AI

Description

Primer AI is an advanced artificial intelligence platform designed for automated news summarization, real-time event monitoring, and information extraction. It helps journalists and analysts quickly understand large volumes of news and data.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, Primer is a leader in natural language processing and AI-driven information analysis, serving media, government, and enterprise clients worldwide.

Key Features

  • Automated news and document summarization
  • Real-time event detection and monitoring
  • Entity and topic extraction
  • Customizable alerting and dashboards
  • API and integration support

How to Use

  • Request a demo or sign up at primer.ai.
  • Configure news sources and topics of interest.
  • Receive automated summaries and alerts for breaking news.
  • Integrate with newsroom or analytics workflows via API.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on organization size and needs
