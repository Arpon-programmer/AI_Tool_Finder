AdCreative.ai

Description

AdCreative.ai is an AI-powered platform that generates high-performing ad creatives and social media posts. It helps marketers and businesses create conversion-focused visuals and copy for ads on Facebook, Google, Instagram, and more.

Company & History

AdCreative.ai was founded in 2021 to help businesses improve their digital advertising results using artificial intelligence. The platform is used by thousands of marketers and agencies worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Starter: For individuals and small businesses.
  • Premium: For agencies and larger teams.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at adcreative.ai.
  • Connect your ad accounts and select your campaign goal.
  • Let the AI generate ad creatives and copy.
  • Download or push creatives directly to your ad platforms.

Key Features

  • AI-generated ad visuals and copy
  • Performance prediction and scoring
  • Integration with major ad platforms
  • Collaboration tools for teams
  • Creative insights and analytics

Pricing

  • Starter: From $29/month
  • Premium: From $189/month
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
