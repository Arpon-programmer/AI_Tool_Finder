Kensho AI

Description

Kensho AI provides advanced analytics and machine learning solutions for financial services, government, and enterprise clients. It specializes in natural language processing, data linking, and knowledge graph technologies to extract insights from complex data.

Company & History

Kensho Technologies, founded in 2013 and acquired by S&P Global in 2018, is a leader in AI-driven analytics for finance and business intelligence.

Key Features

  • Natural language search and analytics
  • Entity linking and knowledge graphs
  • Automated document analysis
  • Financial data enrichment
  • Custom AI solutions for enterprises

How to Use

  • Contact Kensho for enterprise solutions at kensho.com.
  • Integrate APIs and analytics tools into your workflow.
  • Leverage NLP and data linking for business intelligence.
