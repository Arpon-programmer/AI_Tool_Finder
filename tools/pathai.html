PathAI

Description

PathAI develops AI-powered pathology solutions to assist pathologists in diagnosing diseases more accurately and efficiently. Their platform uses machine learning to analyze pathology slides, supporting cancer diagnosis, drug development, and research.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, PathAI partners with leading healthcare organizations and pharmaceutical companies to advance digital pathology and improve patient outcomes through AI-driven insights.

Key Solutions

  • AI-assisted pathology slide analysis
  • Diagnostic support for cancer and other diseases
  • Clinical trial and drug development support
  • Digital pathology workflow integration

How to Use

  • Contact PathAI for a demo or partnership.
  • Integrate PathAI solutions into pathology labs or research workflows.
  • Leverage AI insights for diagnostics and research.

Key Features

  • High-accuracy AI models for pathology
  • Cloud-based and on-premise deployment
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Collaboration tools for pathologists

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on deployment and usage
