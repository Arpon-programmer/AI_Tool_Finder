ChatGPT

Description

ChatGPT is an advanced conversational AI developed by OpenAI. It can answer questions, generate text, assist with coding, and much more, using state-of-the-art large language models (GPT-3.5, GPT-4, and beyond).

Company & History

ChatGPT was launched by OpenAI in November 2022 and quickly became the most popular AI chatbot worldwide. It is based on the GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) architecture, with continuous improvements and new versions released regularly.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Access to GPT-3.5 with basic features.
  • Plus: $20/month, access to GPT-4, faster responses, and priority access to new features.
  • Team/Enterprise: Advanced collaboration, admin controls, and custom pricing.

How to Use

  • Sign up at chat.openai.com.
  • Start a new chat and enter your questions or prompts.
  • Use for coding help, writing, brainstorming, and more.
  • Upgrade to Plus for advanced features and GPT-4 access.

Key Features

  • Conversational AI for any topic
  • Code generation and debugging
  • Text summarization, rewriting, and translation
  • Plugin and web browsing support (Plus/Enterprise)
  • Custom GPTs and integrations

Pricing

  • Free: Basic access
  • Plus: $20/month
  • Team/Enterprise: Custom pricing
