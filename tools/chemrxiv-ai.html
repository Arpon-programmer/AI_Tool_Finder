ChemRxiv AI

Description

ChemRxiv AI is an AI-powered platform for searching and analyzing chemistry preprints, helping researchers stay updated with the latest scientific discoveries.

Company & History

ChemRxiv is a preprint server for the global chemistry community, and its AI features enhance literature discovery and research insights.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Open access to preprints and AI tools.

How to Use

  • Visit chemrxiv.org.
  • Search for chemistry preprints using AI-powered tools.
  • Analyze trends and topics in chemical research.
  • Download and share preprints.

Key Features

  • AI-powered literature search
  • Research trend analysis
  • Open access to preprints
  • Collaboration and sharing tools
