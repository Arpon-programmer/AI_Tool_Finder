Inworld AI

Description

Inworld AI provides advanced AI for creating lifelike NPCs and game dialogue, enabling immersive and dynamic game experiences.

Company & History

Founded in 2021, Inworld AI is a leader in generative AI for games, trusted by studios worldwide for NPC and character AI.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited usage and features.
  • Pro: Advanced features and higher usage limits.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions and support.

How to Use

  • Sign up at inworld.ai.
  • Create and configure AI characters.
  • Integrate with your game engine (Unity, Unreal, etc.).
  • Test and deploy interactive NPCs in your game.

Key Features

  • AI-powered NPC dialogue and behavior
  • Integration with major game engines
  • Customizable personalities and backstories
  • Real-time interaction and adaptation

Pricing

  • Free: Limited access
  • Pro: Contact for pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
