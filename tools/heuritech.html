Heuritech

Description

Heuritech is an AI trend forecasting and consumer insights platform for fashion brands, using image recognition and social media analytics to predict trends.

Company & History

Founded in 2013, Heuritech is a Paris-based leader in AI-driven fashion analytics, serving global luxury and retail brands.

Key Features

  • AI-powered trend forecasting
  • Consumer insights from social media
  • Image recognition for fashion analysis
  • Market segmentation and reporting
  • Custom dashboards and alerts

How to Use

  • Request a demo at heuritech.com.
  • Access trend reports and dashboards.
  • Integrate insights into your design and marketing workflow.

Pricing
