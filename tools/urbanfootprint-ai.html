UrbanFootprint AI

Description

UrbanFootprint AI is a geospatial analytics platform that uses AI to model, analyze, and visualize urban environments for planning, sustainability, and resilience.

Company & History

Founded in 2014, UrbanFootprint provides data-driven solutions for urban planners, governments, and organizations to support smarter city planning and policy decisions.

Versions & Plans

  • Professional: Full-featured platform for planning professionals.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions and integrations for large organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at urbanfootprint.com.
  • Import or select geospatial data for your region.
  • Use AI-powered tools to analyze, model, and visualize scenarios.
  • Export reports and share insights with stakeholders.

Key Features

  • AI-powered geospatial analytics
  • Scenario modeling and visualization
  • Data integration and reporting
  • Collaboration tools for planning teams
