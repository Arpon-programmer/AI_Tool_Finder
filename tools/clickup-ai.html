Description

ClickUp AI brings artificial intelligence to productivity and project management, automating tasks, generating summaries, and providing smart suggestions to boost team efficiency.

Company & History

ClickUp, founded in 2017, is a leading productivity platform. Its AI features help teams work smarter and automate repetitive work.
