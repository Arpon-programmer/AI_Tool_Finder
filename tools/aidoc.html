Aidoc

Description

Aidoc is an AI-powered radiology platform that helps medical professionals detect and prioritize critical conditions in medical images. It provides real-time clinical insights to improve patient care and workflow efficiency in hospitals and imaging centers.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, Aidoc is a leader in AI for medical imaging, serving healthcare providers globally with FDA-cleared solutions for a wide range of acute and chronic conditions.

Key Solutions

  • AI triage and notification for radiology
  • Automated detection of critical findings
  • Workflow integration with PACS/RIS
  • Comprehensive analytics dashboard

How to Use

  • Contact Aidoc for a demo or trial.
  • Integrate Aidoc with your radiology workflow.
  • Receive AI-powered alerts and insights in real time.

Key Features

  • Seamless workflow integration
  • Cloud-based and on-premise options
