Freeletics AI

Description

Freeletics AI is a digital fitness coach that uses artificial intelligence to create personalized workout plans and adapt training based on user progress and feedback.

Company & History

Founded in 2013 in Germany, Freeletics is a leading fitness technology company focused on AI-driven personal training and digital health solutions.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic workouts and features.
  • Coach: Personalized AI coaching and advanced plans (subscription).

How to Use

  • Download the Freeletics app from freeletics.com.
  • Set your fitness goals and preferences.
  • Follow AI-generated workout plans and track your progress.

Key Features

  • AI-powered personalized workouts
  • Progressive training adaptation
  • Bodyweight and equipment-based exercises
  • Motivational coaching and feedback

Pricing

  • Free: Basic features
  • Coach: Subscription required
