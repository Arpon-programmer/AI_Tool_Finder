UiPath

Description

UiPath is an AI and RPA (Robotic Process Automation) platform for business process automation, enabling organizations to automate repetitive tasks and workflows with AI-powered bots.

Company & History

Founded in 2005, UiPath is a global leader in RPA and AI automation, serving enterprises across industries.

Key Features

  • AI-powered robotic process automation
  • Workflow design and automation
  • Integration with business applications
  • Analytics and reporting
  • Marketplace for automation components
