Magisto

Description

Magisto is an AI-powered video editing platform that automatically creates polished videos from raw footage. It uses AI to analyze, edit, and produce videos for marketing, social media, and personal use.

Company & History

Magisto was founded in 2009 and acquired by Vimeo in 2019. It is popular among marketers, small businesses, and content creators for its automated video editing capabilities.

Key Features

  • AI-driven video editing and production
  • Automatic scene detection and transitions
  • Music and effects integration
  • Templates for marketing and social media
  • Easy sharing and export options

How to Use

  • Sign up at magisto.com.
  • Upload your video clips and select a template.
  • Let Magisto automatically edit and produce your video.
  • Download or share your finished video.

Pricing

  • Free trial available
  • Subscription plans for advanced features
