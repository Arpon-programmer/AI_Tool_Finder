PRophet AI

Description

PRophet AI is an AI-driven media outreach and pitch prediction platform for public relations professionals, helping optimize media targeting and improve campaign outcomes.

Company & History

PRophet is a leading AI platform for PR, founded to bring data-driven insights and automation to media relations and communications.
