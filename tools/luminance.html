Luminance

Description

Luminance is an AI-powered legal document review and analysis platform. It helps law firms and in-house teams automate contract review, due diligence, and compliance processes using advanced machine learning.

Company & History

Founded in 2015 in the UK, Luminance is used by thousands of lawyers worldwide for legal document automation and analysis.

Versions & Plans

  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for legal teams and organizations.

How to Use

  • Request a demo at luminance.com.
  • Upload legal documents for review.
  • Use AI tools to analyze, tag, and summarize content.
  • Export or share results with your team.

Key Features

  • AI contract review and due diligence
  • Automated compliance checks
  • Document tagging and summarization
  • Collaboration tools for legal teams

Pricing

  • Custom enterprise pricing
