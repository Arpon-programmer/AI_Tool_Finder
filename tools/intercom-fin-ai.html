Intercom Fin AI

Description

Intercom Fin AI is an AI-powered customer support bot that automates answers to customer questions, resolves issues instantly, and reduces support volume for businesses using the Intercom platform.

Company & History

Intercom, founded in 2011 and based in San Francisco, is a leading provider of customer messaging and support solutions. Fin AI is their advanced AI bot for customer service automation.

Key Features

  • AI-powered automated support bot
  • Instant answers to customer questions
  • Seamless escalation to human agents
  • Integration with Intercom Inbox and Help Center
  • Analytics and reporting

How to Use

  • Enable Fin AI in your Intercom workspace at intercom.com/fin.
  • Configure knowledge base and automation rules.
  • Monitor conversations and optimize bot performance.

Pricing

  • Usage-based pricing
