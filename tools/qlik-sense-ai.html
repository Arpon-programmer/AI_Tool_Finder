Qlik Sense AI

Description

Qlik Sense AI is an analytics platform that leverages artificial intelligence to assist users in data exploration, visualization, and decision-making. It provides augmented analytics and natural language interaction for business intelligence.

Company & History

Qlik, founded in 1993 and headquartered in Pennsylvania, is a global leader in data analytics. Qlik Sense AI extends its platform with advanced AI and machine learning features.

Key Features

  • AI-powered data exploration and insights
  • Augmented analytics and recommendations
  • Natural language interaction
  • Smart visualizations and dashboards
  • Integration with cloud and on-premise data sources

How to Use

  • Sign up at qlik.com.
  • Connect your data and build interactive dashboards.
  • Use AI features for data analysis and insights.
  • Share reports and collaborate with your team.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on business needs
  • Free trial available
