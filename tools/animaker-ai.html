Animaker AI

Description

Animaker AI is an online animation platform that uses artificial intelligence to automate video creation, character animation, and voiceovers. It helps users create professional-quality animated videos quickly and easily.

Company & History

Animaker was founded in 2014 in India. It is used by millions of marketers, educators, and businesses worldwide for animated video production.

Key Features

  • AI-powered video and character animation
  • Automated voiceovers and lip-sync
  • Drag-and-drop video editor
  • Extensive template library
  • Export to multiple formats

How to Use

  • Sign up at animaker.com.
  • Choose a template or start from scratch.
  • Use AI tools to animate characters and add voiceovers.
  • Export and share your video.

Pricing

  • Free plan available
  • Paid plans with advanced features
