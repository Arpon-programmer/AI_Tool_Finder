MarketMuse

Description

MarketMuse is an AI-powered content research and optimization platform. It helps marketers and writers plan, research, and optimize content to improve search rankings and authority by analyzing top-performing content and identifying gaps.

Company & History

Founded in 2013, MarketMuse uses artificial intelligence to automate content audits, topic modeling, and content briefs. It is trusted by agencies, publishers, and enterprises to drive organic growth.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited queries and features.
  • Standard: More queries, content briefs, and recommendations.
  • Premium: Advanced features for teams and agencies.

How to Use

  • Sign up at marketmuse.com.
  • Enter your topic or URL to analyze content opportunities.
  • Review AI-generated content briefs and recommendations.
  • Optimize your content using MarketMuse suggestions.

Key Features

  • AI-driven content research and topic modeling
  • Content briefs and optimization suggestions
  • Competitive analysis and gap identification
  • Content scoring and SERP analysis
  • Integrations with CMS and publishing tools

Pricing

  • Free: Limited access
  • Standard: $149/month
  • Premium: Custom pricing
