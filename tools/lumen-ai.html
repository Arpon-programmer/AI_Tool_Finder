Lumen AI

Description

Lumen AI is an AI-powered platform for metabolic health and nutrition, providing personalized meal and lifestyle recommendations based on real-time metabolic data.

Company & History

Lumen, founded in 2014, uses AI and a handheld device to analyze metabolism and help users optimize nutrition and fitness for their goals.

Versions & Plans

  • Basic: App access with device purchase.
  • Pro: Advanced analytics and coaching.

How to Use

  • Purchase the Lumen device at lumen.me.
  • Connect the device to the app and measure metabolism.
  • Receive AI-driven nutrition and lifestyle recommendations.

Key Features

  • AI-powered metabolic analysis
  • Personalized nutrition and fitness plans
  • Real-time data tracking
  • Progress analytics and coaching

Pricing

  • Basic: Device purchase required
  • Pro: Subscription for advanced features
