Animalytics

Description

Animalytics is an AI-powered analytics platform for animal health, providing veterinarians and researchers with insights from animal data to improve diagnostics, treatment, and welfare.

Company & History

Animalytics was founded to bring advanced data analytics and AI to veterinary medicine, supporting clinics, farms, and research institutions worldwide.

Key Features

  • Animal health data analytics
  • Predictive diagnostics and alerts
  • Integration with veterinary and farm management systems
  • Customizable dashboards and reports
  • Research and population health tools

How to Use

  • Sign up at animalytics.ai.
  • Connect your animal health data sources.
  • Use dashboards and analytics to monitor and improve animal health.

Pricing

  • Subscription: Monthly and annual plans for clinics and farms
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for research and large organizations
