Yummly AI

Description

Yummly AI is an intelligent meal planning and recipe recommendation platform that uses AI to suggest personalized recipes, meal plans, and cooking tips.

Company & History

Yummly, founded in 2009 and acquired by Whirlpool, leverages AI to help users discover and prepare meals tailored to their tastes and dietary needs.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic recipe search and meal planning.
  • Pro: Advanced features, guided cooking, and premium content.

How to Use

  • Visit yummly.com.
  • Search for recipes or set dietary preferences.
  • Get personalized meal plans and cooking tips.

Key Features

  • AI-powered recipe recommendations
  • Personalized meal planning
  • Guided cooking and video tutorials
  • Shopping list and pantry management

Pricing

  • Free: Basic features
  • Pro: Contact for pricing
