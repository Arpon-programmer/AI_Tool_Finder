Scenario.gg

Description

Scenario.gg is an AI-powered platform for generating custom game assets, including characters, props, and environments, tailored for game development.

Company & History

Founded in 2021, Scenario.gg is trusted by indie and AAA studios for rapid, high-quality asset generation using generative AI.

Versions & Plans

  • Starter: Basic asset generation features.
  • Pro: Advanced tools and higher limits.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions and support.

How to Use

  • Sign up at scenario.com.
  • Upload references or describe your asset needs.
  • Use AI to generate and refine assets.
  • Download and integrate assets into your game.

Key Features

  • AI-generated game assets
  • Customizable styles and outputs
  • Batch generation and upscaling
  • Commercial usage rights

Pricing

  • Starter: Free trial available
  • Pro: Subscription pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
