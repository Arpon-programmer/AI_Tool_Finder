Amper Music

Description

Amper Music is an AI music generator that enables users to create royalty-free tracks and soundscapes for videos, games, and other creative projects.

Company & History

Founded in 2014, Amper Music is a pioneer in AI-generated music, serving creators and businesses worldwide.

Key Features

  • AI-generated music in various styles
  • Customizable tempo, mood, and instrumentation
  • Instant download and integration
  • Royalty-free licensing
  • API for developers

How to Use

  • Sign up at ampermusic.com.
  • Choose your music parameters and generate a track.
  • Download and use in your project.

Pricing

  • Free and paid plans available
