Ahrefs AI

Description

Ahrefs AI brings artificial intelligence to the Ahrefs SEO platform, providing advanced tools for keyword research, backlink analysis, content optimization, and competitor tracking. It helps SEO specialists and marketers improve website rankings and visibility.

Company & History

Ahrefs was founded in 2010 and is a leading provider of SEO tools. Its AI features are used by professionals worldwide to drive organic growth.

Key Features

  • AI-powered keyword and content suggestions
  • Backlink and competitor analysis
  • Site audit and health monitoring
  • Rank tracking and reporting
  • Content gap analysis

How to Use

  • Sign up at ahrefs.com.
  • Add your website or project.
  • Use AI tools for keyword, content, and backlink analysis.
  • Monitor rankings and optimize your SEO strategy.

Pricing

  • Free trial available
  • Subscription-based plans
