Drift AI

Description

Drift AI is a conversational marketing and sales platform that uses artificial intelligence to automate chat, qualify leads, and book meetings. It helps sales teams engage website visitors in real time and accelerate the sales process.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, Drift is based in Boston and is a pioneer in conversational marketing and sales automation for B2B companies.

Key Features

  • AI-powered chatbots and live chat
  • Lead qualification and meeting booking
  • Personalized messaging and targeting
  • Integration with CRM and sales tools
  • Analytics and reporting

How to Use

  • Sign up at drift.com and install the Drift widget on your website.
  • Configure chatbots and lead routing rules.
  • Engage visitors and qualify leads automatically.
  • Book meetings and sync with your sales calendar.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on business needs
