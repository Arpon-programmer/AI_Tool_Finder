Promethean AI

Description

Promethean AI assists game developers in building virtual worlds using AI-driven tools for environment creation and asset management.

Company & History

Founded in 2018, Promethean AI is a pioneer in AI-powered world building, used by leading studios for faster and more creative workflows.

Versions & Plans

  • Indie: Free for small teams and personal use.
  • Studio: Paid plan for professional teams.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions and support.

How to Use

  • Request access at prometheanai.com.
  • Install the Promethean AI plugin for your 3D software.
  • Use AI tools to generate and organize environments.
  • Iterate and export assets for your game engine.

Key Features

  • AI-assisted world building
  • Asset management and organization
  • Integration with 3D software
  • Collaboration tools

Pricing

  • Indie: Free
  • Studio: Contact for pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
