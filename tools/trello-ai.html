Trello AI

Description

Trello AI enhances the Trello project management platform with artificial intelligence features for task automation, smart suggestions, and workflow optimization, making team collaboration more efficient.

Company & History

Trello, launched in 2011 and now part of Atlassian, is a popular visual collaboration tool. Its AI features help teams automate repetitive tasks and manage projects smarter.

Key Features

  • AI-powered automation (Butler)
  • Smart card suggestions
  • Workflow optimization
  • Integration with other Atlassian tools
  • Custom rule creation

How to Use

  • Enable Butler automation in your Trello boards at trello.com/platforms/automation.
  • Set up custom rules and commands for your workflow.
  • Leverage AI suggestions to optimize project management.

Pricing

  • Included in select Trello plans
  • Additional costs for advanced automation
