Buoy Health

Description

Buoy Health is an AI-powered digital health assistant that helps users assess symptoms, find care options, and navigate the healthcare system. It uses advanced algorithms to provide personalized health guidance and connect users to appropriate care resources.

Company & History

Founded in 2014, Buoy Health partners with healthcare providers, insurers, and employers to improve patient engagement and reduce unnecessary healthcare costs through intelligent triage and navigation tools.

Key Solutions

  • AI symptom checker
  • Personalized care recommendations
  • Healthcare navigation and triage
  • Integration with provider networks

How to Use

  • Access Buoy Health via their website or partner portals.
  • Enter your symptoms and answer guided questions.
  • Receive personalized care recommendations and next steps.

Key Features

  • Conversational AI interface
  • Evidence-based recommendations
  • Integration with healthcare systems
  • Data privacy and security

Pricing

  • Free for individual users
  • Custom pricing for enterprise solutions
