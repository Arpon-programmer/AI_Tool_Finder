Foodvisor AI

Description

Foodvisor AI is an AI-powered nutrition tracking and analysis app that uses image recognition and data science to provide instant food logging, calorie counting, and personalized meal recommendations.

Company & History

Founded in France in 2015, Foodvisor leverages AI to help users achieve their health and nutrition goals through smart food tracking and coaching.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic food logging and nutrition tracking.
  • Premium: Personalized coaching and advanced features.

How to Use

  • Download the app from foodvisor.io.
  • Log meals using photo recognition or manual entry.
  • Track nutrition, set goals, and receive personalized advice.

Key Features

  • AI-powered food recognition
  • Calorie and nutrition tracking
  • Personalized meal recommendations
  • Coaching and progress analytics

Pricing

  • Free: Basic features
  • Premium: Subscription required
