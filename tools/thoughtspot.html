ThoughtSpot

Description

ThoughtSpot is an AI-powered analytics platform that enables users to search, analyze, and visualize data using natural language queries. It delivers instant insights and self-service analytics for business users.

Company & History

Founded in 2012 and based in California, ThoughtSpot is a leader in search-driven analytics and business intelligence, empowering organizations to make data-driven decisions.

Key Features

  • AI-powered search and analytics
  • Natural language query interface
  • Automated insights and recommendations
  • Interactive dashboards and visualizations
  • Integration with cloud data platforms

How to Use

  • Sign up at thoughtspot.com.
  • Connect your data sources and start searching for insights.
  • Use natural language queries to analyze and visualize data.
  • Share dashboards and insights with your team.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on organization needs
  • Free trial available
