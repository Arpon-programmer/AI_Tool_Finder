SciNote AI

Description

SciNote AI is an electronic lab notebook platform with AI-powered features for managing scientific experiments, data, and research workflows.

Company & History

SciNote was founded in 2015 and is used by scientists and research organizations worldwide for digital lab management and automation.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic ELN features for individuals and small teams.
  • Premium: Advanced features, integrations, and support.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at scinote.net.
  • Create and organize lab projects and experiments.
  • Use AI features for data analysis and reporting.
  • Collaborate with team members and export results.

Key Features

  • Electronic lab notebook (ELN)
  • AI-powered data analysis
  • Project and workflow management
  • Collaboration and integrations

Pricing

  • Free: Basic access
  • Premium: Contact for pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
