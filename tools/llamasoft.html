Llamasoft

Description

Llamasoft is an AI-powered supply chain analytics and optimization platform, enabling organizations to model, analyze, and optimize their supply chain operations for efficiency and resilience.

Company & History

Founded in 1998 and acquired by Coupa in 2020, Llamasoft is a global leader in supply chain AI and decision intelligence.

Key Features

  • AI-driven supply chain modeling and simulation
  • Scenario planning and risk analysis
  • Demand forecasting and inventory optimization
  • Network design and logistics optimization
  • Integration with enterprise systems

How to Use

  • Request a demo at llamasoft.com.
  • Integrate with your supply chain data sources.
  • Model, analyze, and optimize supply chain scenarios.

Pricing
