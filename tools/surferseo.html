SurferSEO

Description

SurferSEO is an AI-powered SEO tool that helps content creators and marketers optimize their web pages for higher search engine rankings. It provides data-driven recommendations for on-page SEO, keyword usage, and content structure.

Company & History

Founded in 2017, SurferSEO is used by thousands of SEO professionals and agencies worldwide. It leverages machine learning and SERP analysis to deliver actionable insights for content optimization.

Versions & Plans

  • Essential: For individuals and small teams.
  • Advanced: For growing businesses and agencies.
  • Max: For large teams and enterprises.

How to Use

  • Sign up at surferseo.com.
  • Enter your target keyword and URL to analyze your page.
  • Follow SurferSEO's recommendations to optimize your content.
  • Track your rankings and content performance over time.

Key Features

  • On-page SEO analysis and recommendations
  • Content editor with real-time scoring
  • Keyword research and SERP analysis
  • Audit and backlink tools
  • Integrations with Google Docs and WordPress

Pricing

  • Advanced: $179/month
  • Max: $299/month
