Designify

Description

Designify is an AI tool for automating fashion design visuals, mockups, and product images, helping designers create professional visuals quickly.

Company & History

Launched by the creators of Remove.bg, Designify leverages AI for creative automation in fashion and e-commerce.

Key Features

  • AI-powered background removal and enhancement
  • Batch processing for product images
  • Customizable templates and mockups
  • API for automation
  • High-resolution exports

How to Use

  • Go to designify.com.
  • Upload your design or product images.
  • Apply AI enhancements and download results.

Pricing

  • Free and paid plans available
