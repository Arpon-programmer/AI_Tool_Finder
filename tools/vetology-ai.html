Vetology AI

Description

Vetology AI is an AI-powered veterinary radiology platform that provides automated X-ray analysis and diagnostic support for veterinarians. It helps clinics improve accuracy, speed, and efficiency in animal healthcare.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, Vetology AI is a leader in veterinary imaging AI, serving clinics and hospitals worldwide with advanced diagnostic tools.

Key Features

  • Automated X-ray interpretation
  • Instant diagnostic reports
  • Integration with veterinary practice management systems
  • Cloud-based image storage and sharing
  • Continuous AI model updates

How to Use

  • Sign up at vetology.ai.
  • Upload X-ray images for instant AI analysis.
  • Review diagnostic reports and share with your team or clients.

Pricing

  • Subscription: Monthly and annual plans for clinics
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large organizations
