Description

Planhat is an AI-powered customer insights and workflow automation platform, enabling customer success teams to manage accounts, automate tasks, and drive engagement with actionable analytics.

Company & History

Founded in 2014, Planhat is a global provider of customer success and analytics solutions for SaaS and enterprise businesses.

Key Features

  • AI-driven customer insights and analytics
  • Workflow automation and playbooks
  • Customer health scoring and segmentation
  • Engagement tracking and alerts
  • CRM and support tool integrations
