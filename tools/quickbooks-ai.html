QuickBooks AI

Description

QuickBooks AI is the artificial intelligence-powered feature set within QuickBooks, Intuit's accounting software. It automates bookkeeping, categorizes transactions, predicts cash flow, and provides smart insights for small businesses and accountants.

Company & History

Intuit, founded in 1983, has integrated AI and machine learning into QuickBooks to help automate and simplify accounting tasks for millions of users worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Simple Start: Basic accounting and AI features.
  • Essentials: More users and advanced automation.
  • Plus: Project tracking and inventory.
  • Advanced: Premium AI insights and analytics.

How to Use

  • Sign up at quickbooks.intuit.com.
  • Connect your bank and business accounts.
  • Let QuickBooks AI categorize and analyze transactions.
  • Use dashboards for insights and cash flow predictions.

Key Features

  • Automated transaction categorization
  • Cash flow prediction
  • Smart invoicing and expense tracking
  • AI-powered insights and analytics

Pricing

  • Essentials: $60/month
  • Plus: $90/month
  • Advanced: $200/month
