Siemens EDA AI

Description

Siemens EDA AI brings artificial intelligence to electronic design automation, enabling smarter, faster, and more reliable chip and PCB design.

Company & History

Siemens EDA (formerly Mentor Graphics) is a leader in EDA software, integrating AI to advance electronic and semiconductor design.

Key Features

  • AI-driven design rule checking
  • Automated layout and routing
  • Predictive analytics for design errors
  • Integration with Siemens EDA tools
  • Cloud-based collaboration

How to Use
