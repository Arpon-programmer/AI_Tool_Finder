Blue Yonder

Description

Blue Yonder is an AI and machine learning platform for end-to-end supply chain optimization, providing demand forecasting, inventory management, and logistics solutions for enterprises.

Company & History

Founded in 2008 and acquired by Panasonic in 2021, Blue Yonder is a global leader in digital supply chain transformation and AI-driven logistics.

Key Features

  • AI-powered demand and supply planning
  • Inventory and logistics optimization
  • Real-time analytics and dashboards
  • Collaboration and workflow automation
  • Integration with ERP and enterprise systems

How to Use

  • Request a demo at blueyonder.com.
  • Integrate with your supply chain and ERP data.
  • Leverage AI for planning, forecasting, and optimization.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing for enterprise clients
