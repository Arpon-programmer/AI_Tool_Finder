Freshdesk AI

Description

Freshdesk AI brings artificial intelligence and automation to the Freshdesk customer support platform, enabling businesses to resolve tickets faster, automate repetitive tasks, and deliver better customer experiences.

Company & History

Freshdesk, launched in 2010 by Freshworks, is a leading cloud-based customer support software. Its AI features help businesses scale support and improve efficiency.

Key Features

  • AI-powered ticket assignment and resolution
  • Automated responses and chatbots
  • Sentiment analysis and intent detection
  • Self-service and knowledge base integration
  • Analytics and reporting

How to Use

  • Enable AI features in your Freshdesk account at freshdesk.com/ai.
  • Configure automation rules and chatbots.
  • Monitor ticket resolution and optimize AI workflows.

Pricing

  • Included in select Freshdesk plans
  • Additional costs for advanced AI features
