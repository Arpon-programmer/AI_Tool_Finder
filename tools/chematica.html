Chematica

Description

Chematica is an AI-powered platform for synthetic route planning, enabling chemists to design efficient and innovative chemical syntheses.

Company & History

Chematica, now part of Merck KGaA (SynFini), was developed by leading chemists and computer scientists to revolutionize organic synthesis planning.

Versions & Plans

  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for research organizations and industry.

How to Use

  • Contact SynFini via synfini.com for access to Chematica.
  • Input target molecules and constraints.
  • Use AI to generate and evaluate synthetic routes.
  • Export synthesis plans for laboratory execution.

Key Features

  • AI-driven synthetic route planning
  • Extensive reaction database
  • Customizable synthesis constraints
  • Integration with lab automation
