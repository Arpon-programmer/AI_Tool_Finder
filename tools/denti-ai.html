Denti.AI

Description

Denti.AI is an AI-powered dental charting and analysis platform that automates dental X-ray interpretation, charting, and workflow for dental practices, improving efficiency and diagnostic accuracy.

Company & History

Denti.AI was founded in 2017 and is trusted by dental clinics and DSOs for its innovative AI dental solutions and seamless integration with practice management systems.

Key Features

  • AI-powered dental X-ray analysis
  • Automated dental charting
  • Integration with dental practice management software
  • Cloud-based reporting and analytics
  • Continuous AI model updates

How to Use

  • Sign up at denti.ai.
  • Upload dental X-rays for instant AI analysis and charting.
  • Review results and integrate with your clinical workflow.

Pricing

  • Subscription: Monthly and annual plans for dental practices
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large organizations
