Casetext CoCounsel

Description

Casetext CoCounsel is an AI legal assistant that helps lawyers with legal research, document review, deposition preparation, and contract analysis. It leverages advanced language models to automate and streamline legal workflows.

Company & History

Casetext, founded in 2013, is a legal technology company acquired by Thomson Reuters in 2023. CoCounsel is their flagship AI product for legal professionals.

Versions & Plans

  • Subscription: Monthly or annual plans for law firms and enterprises.

How to Use

  • Sign up at casetext.com.
  • Access CoCounsel from the dashboard.
  • Upload documents or enter legal queries.
  • Review AI-generated research and analysis.

Key Features

  • AI-powered legal research
  • Document review and summarization
  • Deposition and contract analysis
  • Integrations with legal databases

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on firm size and needs
