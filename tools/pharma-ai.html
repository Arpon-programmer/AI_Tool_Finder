Pharma.AI

Description

Pharma.AI is an AI platform for drug discovery, pharmacy, and pharmaceutical research. It accelerates drug development, optimizes compound selection, and provides insights for pharmacists and researchers.

Company & History

Pharma.AI is developed by Insilico Medicine, a leader in AI-driven drug discovery and healthcare innovation since 2014.

Key Features

  • AI-powered drug discovery and design
  • Compound screening and optimization
  • Pharmacogenomics and personalized medicine
  • Integration with pharma R&D workflows
  • Data analytics and visualization

How to Use

  • Request access at pharma.ai.
  • Upload or select compounds for analysis and optimization.
  • Use AI tools for research, discovery, and reporting.

Pricing

  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for pharma and research organizations
