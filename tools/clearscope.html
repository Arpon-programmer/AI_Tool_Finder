Clearscope

Description

Clearscope is an AI-powered content optimization platform for SEO specialists and content marketers. It analyzes top-performing content and provides keyword and topic recommendations to help you create high-ranking, comprehensive articles.

Company & History

Clearscope was founded in 2016 in the United States. It is trusted by leading brands and agencies for improving content quality and search visibility.

Key Features

  • AI-driven content analysis and optimization
  • Keyword and topic recommendations
  • Content grading and scoring
  • Integration with Google Docs and WordPress
  • Reporting and collaboration tools

How to Use

  • Sign up at clearscope.io.
  • Start a new content report for your target keyword.
  • Use AI recommendations to optimize your content.
  • Export or publish your optimized content.

Pricing

  • Subscription-based pricing
  • Free trial available
