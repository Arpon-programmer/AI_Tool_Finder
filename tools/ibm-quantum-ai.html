IBM Quantum AI

Description

IBM Quantum AI provides cloud-based access to quantum computers and AI tools for research in physics, chemistry, and computer science.

Company & History

IBM launched its quantum computing initiative in 2016, making quantum hardware and software available to researchers and developers worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Lite: Free access to select quantum systems.
  • Premium: Priority access, advanced features, and support.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at quantum-computing.ibm.com.
  • Access quantum computers and simulators via the cloud.
  • Use Qiskit and AI tools for quantum research.
  • Collaborate and share results with the community.

Key Features

  • Cloud-based quantum computing
  • AI tools for quantum research
  • Open-source SDK (Qiskit)
  • Community and enterprise support

Pricing

  • Lite: Free
  • Premium: Contact for pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
