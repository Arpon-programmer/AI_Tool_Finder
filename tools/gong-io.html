Gong.io

Description

Gong.io is an AI-powered revenue intelligence platform that analyzes sales calls, meetings, and emails to deliver insights for sales teams. It helps improve sales performance, coaching, and forecasting by capturing and understanding customer interactions.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, Gong.io is headquartered in San Francisco. It is widely used by sales organizations to drive productivity and revenue growth through data-driven insights.

Key Features

  • AI-driven conversation analytics
  • Deal and pipeline insights
  • Automated coaching recommendations
  • Forecasting and trend analysis
  • Integration with CRM and sales tools

How to Use

  • Request a demo or sign up at gong.io.
  • Connect your sales communication tools and CRM.
  • Analyze sales calls, meetings, and emails for actionable insights.
  • Use AI-driven recommendations to improve sales outcomes.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on team size and needs
