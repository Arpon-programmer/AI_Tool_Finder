Magenta Studio

Description

Magenta Studio is a suite of open-source AI tools by Google for music generation, creative workflows, and experimentation with machine learning in music.

Company & History

Developed by Google Research, Magenta Studio is part of the Magenta project, advancing AI in music and art since 2016.

Key Features

  • AI-powered music generation and transformation
  • MIDI and Ableton Live integration
  • Open-source and extensible
  • Creative tools for melody, harmony, and rhythm
  • Community and research resources

How to Use

  • Download from magenta.tensorflow.org/studio.
  • Install as standalone or Ableton Live plugin.
  • Experiment with AI-powered music creation tools.

Pricing

  • Free and open-source
