Ginger AI

Description

Ginger AI is an on-demand mental health platform that uses AI to provide behavioral health coaching, therapy, and psychiatry. It offers 24/7 chat-based support, personalized self-care content, and access to licensed professionals for individuals and organizations.

Company & History

Founded in 2011, Ginger merged with Headspace Health in 2021. The platform is widely used by employers and health plans to support employee mental health and well-being.

Key Features

  • 24/7 AI-powered behavioral health coaching
  • Access to licensed therapists and psychiatrists
  • Personalized self-care content and exercises
  • Confidential chat-based support
  • Integration with employer and health plans

How to Use

  • Download the Ginger app from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Sign up and connect with a behavioral health coach instantly.
  • Access therapy, psychiatry, and self-care resources as needed.

Pricing

  • Enterprise: Available through employers and health plans
