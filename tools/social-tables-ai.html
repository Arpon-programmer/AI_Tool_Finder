Social Tables AI

Description

Social Tables AI is an event diagramming and guest management platform that uses AI to optimize seating, layouts, and collaboration for meetings and events.

Company & History

Founded in 2011 and acquired by Cvent in 2018, Social Tables is a leading provider of event planning software for venues, planners, and hospitality professionals.

Versions & Plans

  • Basic: Free plan with limited features.
  • Professional: Advanced features for event professionals.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large venues and organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at socialtables.com.
  • Create or import a venue layout.
  • Use AI tools to arrange seating, manage guests, and optimize event flow.
  • Collaborate with team members and clients in real time.

Key Features

  • AI-powered event diagramming
  • Seating and layout optimization
  • Guest list and RSVP management
  • Collaboration and sharing tools
