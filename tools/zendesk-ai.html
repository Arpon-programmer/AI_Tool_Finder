Zendesk AI

Description

Zendesk AI brings artificial intelligence and automation to the Zendesk customer support platform, helping businesses resolve tickets faster, automate responses, and improve customer satisfaction.

Company & History

Zendesk, founded in 2007 and based in San Francisco, is a global leader in customer service software. Zendesk AI enhances its platform with advanced automation and analytics.

Key Features

  • AI-powered ticket routing and triage
  • Automated responses and macros
  • Intent detection and sentiment analysis
  • Self-service and knowledge base integration
  • Analytics and reporting

How to Use

  • Enable AI features in your Zendesk account at zendesk.com/ai.
  • Configure automation rules and workflows.
  • Monitor ticket resolution and optimize AI performance.

Pricing

  • Included in select Zendesk plans
  • Additional costs for advanced AI features
