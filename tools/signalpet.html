SignalPET

Description

SignalPET is an AI-powered veterinary radiology platform that analyzes X-rays in real time, providing instant, objective results to support veterinarians in clinical decision-making and improve patient outcomes.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, SignalPET is used by veterinary clinics worldwide and is recognized for its innovative AI technology in animal healthcare diagnostics.

Key Features

  • Real-time X-ray analysis
  • Automated diagnostic reports
  • Integration with practice management systems
  • Continuous AI model improvement
  • Cloud-based access and storage

How to Use

  • Sign up at signalpet.com.
  • Upload X-ray images for instant AI analysis.
  • Review diagnostic results and share with your team or clients.

Pricing

  • Subscription: Monthly and annual plans for clinics
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large organizations
