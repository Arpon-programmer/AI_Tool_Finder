BenchSci

Description

BenchSci uses AI to decode scientific data and accelerate biomedical research, helping scientists find reagents and design experiments more efficiently.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, BenchSci is a Canadian company serving leading pharmaceutical and academic research organizations worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Academic: Free for academic researchers.
  • Enterprise: Paid plan for pharmaceutical and biotech companies.

How to Use

  • Sign up at benchsci.com.
  • Search for antibodies, reagents, and experimental data.
  • Use AI-powered recommendations for experiment design.

Key Features

  • AI-powered reagent search
  • Experiment design assistance
  • Data-driven recommendations
  • Integration with lab workflows

Pricing

  • Academic: Free
  • Enterprise: Contact for pricing
