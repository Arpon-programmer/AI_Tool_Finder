Harvey AI

Description

Harvey AI is an AI platform for legal professionals, providing tools for contract analysis, legal research, and document automation. It uses large language models to assist law firms and in-house legal teams with complex legal tasks.

Company & History

Founded in 2022, Harvey AI has partnered with major law firms and organizations to deliver AI-driven legal solutions and workflow automation.

Versions & Plans

  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for law firms and legal departments.

How to Use

  • Review AI-generated insights and outputs.
