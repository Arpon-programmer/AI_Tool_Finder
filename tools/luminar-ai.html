Luminar AI

Description

Luminar AI is an AI-powered photo editing software designed to simplify and enhance the photo editing process. It offers one-click enhancements, creative tools, and advanced AI features for photographers of all skill levels.

Company & History

Luminar AI is developed by Skylum, a software company founded in 2008. Luminar AI was launched in 2020 as the first fully AI-powered photo editor.

Key Features

  • AI Sky Replacement and Augmented Sky
  • Portrait enhancement tools
  • One-click templates and looks
  • AI Structure and Accent tools
  • Batch processing and export

How to Use

  • Download and install Luminar AI from skylum.com.
  • Import your photos and apply AI-powered enhancements.
  • Use creative tools and templates to edit and export images.

Pricing

  • One-time purchase or subscription
  • Free trial available
