Autodesk Fusion 360 AI

Description

Autodesk Fusion 360 AI brings generative design and machine learning to CAD, enabling mechanical engineers to optimize parts, automate workflows, and accelerate product development.

Company & History

Autodesk is a leader in 3D design, engineering, and entertainment software. Fusion 360 integrates AI to enhance design and engineering productivity.

Key Features

  • AI-powered generative design
  • Automated simulation and optimization
  • Cloud-based collaboration
  • Integrated CAD, CAM, and CAE
  • Design automation tools

How to Use

  • Sign up for Fusion 360 at autodesk.com.
  • Access generative design and AI features from the workspace.
  • Leverage cloud simulation and optimization tools.
