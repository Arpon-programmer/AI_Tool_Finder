Eightfold AI

Description

Eightfold AI is a talent intelligence platform that uses deep learning and AI to help organizations with talent acquisition, management, and retention, matching people to opportunities at scale.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, Eightfold AI is based in California and serves global enterprises with its AI-driven talent solutions.

Versions & Plans

  • Standard: Core talent intelligence features.
  • Enterprise: Advanced analytics, integrations, and support.

How to Use

  • Integrate with your HR systems.
  • Import employee and candidate data.
  • Leverage AI for talent matching and insights.

Key Features

  • AI-powered talent matching
  • Workforce analytics
  • Diversity and bias reduction tools
  • Integration with HR platforms

Pricing

  • Standard: Custom pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
