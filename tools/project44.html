Description

Project44 is a leading AI-powered supply chain visibility platform, providing real-time tracking, predictive analytics, and workflow automation for global logistics and transportation networks.

Founded in 2014, Project44 is a global leader in supply chain visibility, serving shippers, carriers, and logistics providers with advanced AI and data integration solutions.
