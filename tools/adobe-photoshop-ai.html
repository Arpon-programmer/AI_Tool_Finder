Adobe Photoshop AI

Description

Adobe Photoshop AI integrates artificial intelligence features into Adobe Photoshop, enabling advanced image editing, object selection, content-aware fill, neural filters, and generative AI tools for photographers and designers.

Company & History

Adobe Photoshop, first released in 1990, is the industry standard for image editing. Its AI features, powered by Adobe Sensei, bring automation and creativity to photo editing workflows.

Key Features

  • AI-powered object selection and masking
  • Content-aware fill and healing
  • Neural filters and generative fill
  • Sky replacement and portrait enhancements
  • Integration with Adobe Creative Cloud

How to Use

  • Subscribe to Adobe Photoshop at adobe.com.
  • Open images and use AI-powered tools for editing and enhancement.
  • Apply neural filters and generative AI features for creative effects.

Pricing

  • Subscription-based pricing
  • Free trial available
