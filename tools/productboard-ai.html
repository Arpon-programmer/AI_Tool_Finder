Productboard AI

Description

Productboard AI leverages artificial intelligence to help product teams prioritize features, analyze feedback, and automate product management workflows for better decision-making.

Company & History

Productboard, founded in 2014, is a leading product management platform. Its AI features streamline product planning and customer feedback analysis.

Versions & Plans

  • Essentials: Core product management tools.
  • Pro: Advanced AI and automation features.
  • Enterprise: Custom integrations and support.

How to Use

  • Sign up at productboard.com.
  • Import product feedback and feature ideas.
  • Use AI to analyze and prioritize features.
  • Collaborate with your team and stakeholders.

Key Features

  • AI-powered feedback analysis
  • Automated feature prioritization
  • Roadmap generation
  • Integration with popular tools

Pricing

  • Essentials: $20/user/month
  • Pro: $80/user/month
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
