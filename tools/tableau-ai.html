Tableau AI

Description

Tableau AI brings artificial intelligence and machine learning capabilities to the Tableau analytics platform, enabling users to discover insights, make predictions, and automate data analysis with ease.

Company & History

Tableau, founded in 2003 and acquired by Salesforce in 2019, is a leading provider of data visualization and business intelligence software. Tableau AI extends its platform with advanced analytics and AI-driven features.

Key Features

  • AI-powered data insights and explanations
  • Predictive analytics and forecasting
  • Natural language queries (Ask Data)
  • Automated data preparation
  • Integration with Salesforce Einstein AI

How to Use

  • Enable Tableau AI features in your Tableau environment.
  • Use "Ask Data" for natural language queries and insights.
  • Apply predictive analytics to your dashboards and reports.
  • Leverage AI-driven recommendations for data exploration.

Pricing

  • Included in select Tableau plans
  • Additional costs for advanced AI features
