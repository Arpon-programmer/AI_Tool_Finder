Maple AI

Description

Maple AI is a powerful math computation and visualization tool, offering symbolic and numeric solutions, interactive plots, and AI-powered math assistance.

Developed by Maplesoft, Maple has been a leader in mathematical software since 1988, with AI features added in recent versions.
