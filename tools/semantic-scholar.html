Semantic Scholar AI

Description

Semantic Scholar AI is an academic search engine powered by artificial intelligence, designed to help researchers discover relevant scientific literature, summarize papers, and track citations across millions of publications.

Company & History

Semantic Scholar is developed by the Allen Institute for AI (AI2) and launched in 2015. It leverages AI to improve the efficiency and accuracy of academic research discovery.

Key Features

  • AI-powered academic search
  • Paper summarization and highlights
  • Citation tracking and influence scores
  • Author and topic recommendations
  • Open access to millions of papers

How to Use

  • Visit semanticscholar.org.
  • Search for papers, authors, or topics of interest.
  • Use filters and AI-powered recommendations to refine results.
  • Read summaries and track citations.

Pricing

  • Free to use
