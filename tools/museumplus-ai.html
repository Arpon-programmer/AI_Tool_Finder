MuseumPlus AI

Description

MuseumPlus AI is an advanced museum information and workflow management system that leverages artificial intelligence for cataloging, collection management, and visitor engagement.

Company & History

MuseumPlus is developed by zetcom, a global leader in museum software. The AI features help automate data entry, improve search, and personalize visitor experiences.

Versions & Plans

  • Institutional: Subscription for museums and cultural institutions
  • Custom: Tailored solutions for large collections

How to Use

  • Contact zetcom for a demo or subscription.
  • Integrate with your museum's workflows and data.
  • Use AI-driven cataloging and visitor engagement features.

Key Features

  • AI-powered cataloging and metadata enrichment
  • Automated workflow management
  • Personalized visitor engagement
  • Advanced search and reporting

Pricing

  • Institutional subscription (custom pricing)
