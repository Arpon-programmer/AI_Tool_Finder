X0PA AI

Description

X0PA AI is an AI-powered talent acquisition platform that automates and optimizes hiring, using predictive analytics and machine learning to improve quality of hire and reduce bias.

Company & History

X0PA AI, founded in 2017, is based in Singapore and serves global enterprises with its data-driven recruitment solutions.

Versions & Plans

  • Standard: Core AI recruitment tools.
  • Enterprise: Advanced analytics and integrations.

How to Use

  • Set up your hiring workflow in X0PA AI.
  • Post jobs and invite candidates.
  • Leverage AI for screening, ranking, and shortlisting.

Key Features

  • AI-driven candidate screening
  • Predictive analytics for hiring
  • Bias mitigation tools
  • Integration with HR systems
