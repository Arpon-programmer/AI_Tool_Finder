Vic.ai

Description

Vic.ai is an AI platform for accounting and finance teams that automates invoice processing, approval workflows, and provides real-time insights. It leverages machine learning to improve accuracy and efficiency in financial operations.

Company & History

Founded in 2017, Vic.ai is used by enterprises and accounting firms to streamline accounts payable and automate manual finance tasks.

Versions & Plans

  • Core: Invoice automation and approval workflows.
  • Enterprise: Advanced analytics and integrations.

How to Use

  • Request a demo at vic.ai.
  • Integrate with your accounting software.
  • Upload invoices for AI processing.
  • Review and approve transactions in the dashboard.

Key Features

  • AI invoice processing
  • Automated approval workflows
  • Real-time financial insights
  • Integrations with accounting platforms

Pricing
