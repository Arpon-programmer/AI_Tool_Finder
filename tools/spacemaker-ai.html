Spacemaker AI

Description

Spacemaker AI is an AI-powered platform for urban and building design, enabling planners and architects to optimize site development and maximize project value.

Company & History

Founded in 2016 in Norway and acquired by Autodesk in 2020, Spacemaker AI is used globally for data-driven site planning and design.

Versions & Plans

  • Professional: Full-featured platform for design professionals.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at spacemakerai.com.
  • Import site data or select a location.
  • Use AI tools to generate, analyze, and compare design options.
  • Export plans and collaborate with stakeholders.

Key Features

  • AI-driven site and building design
  • Scenario analysis and optimization
  • Data integration and visualization
  • Collaboration and reporting tools
