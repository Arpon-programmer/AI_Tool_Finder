Spacemaker AI

Description

Spacemaker AI is an AI-powered platform for architects and urban planners to design, analyze, and optimize building sites. It helps users generate and evaluate design options quickly, considering factors like sunlight, noise, wind, and regulations.

Company & History

Founded in 2016 in Norway and acquired by Autodesk in 2020, Spacemaker AI is used globally by architects and developers to improve site planning and early-stage design.

Key Features

  • AI-driven site analysis and optimization
  • Automated design generation
  • Environmental impact analysis (sun, wind, noise)
  • Collaboration and sharing tools
  • Integration with Autodesk tools

How to Use

  • Sign up at spacemakerai.com.
  • Upload or define your site and project requirements.
  • Generate and compare design options using AI analysis.
  • Export or share results with your team.
