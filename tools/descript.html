Descript

Description

Descript is an AI-powered video and audio editing platform that allows users to edit media by editing text. It offers transcription, screen recording, podcasting, and video editing with advanced AI features like overdub and filler word removal.

Company & History

Descript was founded in 2017 and is based in San Francisco. It is widely used by podcasters, video editors, and content creators for its innovative approach to media editing.

Key Features

  • Text-based video and audio editing
  • Automatic transcription
  • AI-powered overdub (voice cloning)
  • Screen recording and podcast tools
  • Filler word and silence removal

How to Use

  • Sign up at descript.com.
  • Upload or record your media files.
  • Edit your content by editing the transcript.
  • Export finished audio or video projects.

Pricing

  • Free plan available
  • Paid plans for advanced features
