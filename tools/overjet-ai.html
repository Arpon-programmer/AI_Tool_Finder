Overjet AI

Description

Overjet AI is an AI-powered dental X-ray analysis platform that helps dentists detect and quantify dental diseases, automate charting, and improve clinical decision-making with real-time insights.

Company & History

Founded in 2018 by experts from MIT and Harvard, Overjet is FDA-cleared and used by dental practices, DSOs, and insurers worldwide.

Key Features

  • AI-powered dental X-ray analysis
  • Automated disease detection and charting
  • Integration with dental practice management software
  • Real-time clinical decision support
  • Cloud-based reporting and analytics

How to Use

  • Sign up at overjet.com.
  • Upload dental X-rays for instant AI analysis.
  • Review results and integrate with your clinical workflow.

Pricing

  • Subscription: Monthly and annual plans for dental practices
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large organizations
