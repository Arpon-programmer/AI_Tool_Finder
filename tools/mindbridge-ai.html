MindBridge Ai

Description

MindBridge Ai is an AI-powered financial risk discovery and audit analytics platform. It uses machine learning to analyze financial data, detect anomalies, and help auditors and finance teams identify risks and errors.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, MindBridge Ai is used by auditors, accountants, and finance professionals worldwide to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of audits and financial reviews.

Versions & Plans

  • Ai Auditor: Core audit analytics platform.
  • Ai Financial Risk Discovery: Advanced risk detection and analytics.

How to Use

  • Request a demo at mindbridge.ai.
  • Upload financial data for analysis.
  • Review AI-generated risk scores and reports.
  • Take action based on insights and recommendations.

Key Features

  • AI-driven anomaly detection
  • Automated risk scoring
  • Comprehensive audit analytics
  • Data visualization and reporting

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on organization size and needs
