Wisecut

Description

Wisecut is an AI-powered video editing tool that automatically removes silences, adds background music, and creates jump cuts to make videos more engaging. It is designed for content creators, educators, and marketers who want to save time on editing.

Company & History

Wisecut was founded in 2019 and is based in the United States. It is popular among YouTubers and online educators for its fast, automated editing workflow.

Key Features

  • Automatic silence removal
  • Jump cut creation
  • Background music and audio ducking
  • Auto subtitles and translations
  • AI-powered video summarization

How to Use

  • Sign up at wisecut.video.
  • Upload your video and let Wisecut process it automatically.
  • Review and fine-tune the edits as needed.
  • Download or share your finished video.

Pricing

  • Free plan available
  • Paid plans for advanced features
