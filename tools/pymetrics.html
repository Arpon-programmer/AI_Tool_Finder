Pymetrics

Description

Pymetrics uses neuroscience-based games and AI to match candidates with jobs where they are most likely to succeed, helping companies make fairer, data-driven hiring decisions.

Company & History

Pymetrics, founded in 2013, is a leader in talent matching and soft skills assessment, acquired by Harver in 2022.

Versions & Plans

  • Standard: Core assessments and analytics.
  • Enterprise: Advanced integrations and reporting.

How to Use

  • Invite candidates to complete neuroscience games.
  • Review AI-driven fit scores and recommendations.
  • Integrate with your ATS or HRIS.

Key Features

  • Neuroscience-based assessments
  • AI-powered job matching
  • Bias reduction tools
  • Integration with HR platforms

Pricing

  • Standard: Custom pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
