Kinaxis RapidResponse

Description

Kinaxis RapidResponse is an AI-enhanced supply chain management and scenario planning platform, enabling real-time collaboration, analytics, and decision-making for global supply chains.

Company & History

Founded in 1984, Kinaxis is a Canadian leader in supply chain management software, serving major enterprises worldwide.

Key Features

  • Real-time supply chain planning and analytics
  • AI-driven scenario modeling
  • Collaboration and workflow automation
  • Demand, supply, and inventory management
  • Integration with ERP and enterprise systems

How to Use

  • Request a demo at kinaxis.com.
  • Integrate with your supply chain and ERP data.
  • Leverage AI for scenario planning and optimization.
