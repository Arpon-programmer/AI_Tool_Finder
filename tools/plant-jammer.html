Plant Jammer

Description

Plant Jammer is an AI-powered recipe generator and meal planner that helps chefs and home cooks create plant-based dishes from available ingredients.

Company & History

Founded in Denmark, Plant Jammer aims to make plant-based cooking easy and creative using artificial intelligence and food science.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic recipe generation and meal planning.
  • Premium: Advanced features and integrations.

How to Use

  • Visit plantjammer.com.
  • Enter available ingredients to generate recipes.
  • Customize meals and save favorites.

Key Features

  • AI-powered recipe generation
  • Ingredient-based meal planning
  • Plant-based cooking focus
  • Personalized suggestions

Pricing

  • Free: Basic features
  • Premium: Contact for pricing
