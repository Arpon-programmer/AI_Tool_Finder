Mathpix

Description

Mathpix is an AI-powered tool for converting images and PDFs of math, science, and handwriting into LaTeX, text, and accessible formats.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, Mathpix is used by students, educators, and researchers worldwide for digitizing and editing mathematical content.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited conversions per month.
  • Pro: More conversions, advanced features, and API access.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at mathpix.com.
  • Upload images or PDFs with math content.
  • Convert to LaTeX, text, or other formats.
  • Download or copy the results for use in documents.

Key Features

  • Image-to-LaTeX conversion
  • PDF and handwriting recognition
  • API for developers
  • Multi-language support

Pricing

  • Free: Limited usage
  • Pro: $4.99/month
  • Enterprise: Contact for pricing
