ChurnZero

Description

ChurnZero is an AI platform for churn prediction and customer lifecycle management, helping businesses retain customers and maximize lifetime value through proactive engagement.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, ChurnZero is a leading customer success solution for SaaS and subscription businesses.

Key Features

  • Customer segmentation and health scoring
  • Automated playbooks and workflows
  • Customer journey analytics
  • CRM and support tool integrations
