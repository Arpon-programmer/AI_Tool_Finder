Sensely

Description

Sensely is an AI-powered virtual nurse platform that provides patient triage, symptom checking, and health monitoring. It uses conversational avatars and integrates with healthcare systems to support nurses and care teams.

Company & History

Sensely was founded in 2013 and is used by hospitals, insurers, and clinics worldwide for digital health engagement and patient support.

Key Features

  • AI-powered symptom checker and triage
  • Conversational virtual nurse avatars
  • Remote patient monitoring
  • Integration with EHR and care management systems
  • Multilingual and multi-platform support

How to Use

  • Access Sensely via sensely.com or partner healthcare providers.
  • Patients interact with the virtual nurse for triage and health advice.
  • Healthcare teams use Sensely for patient engagement and monitoring.

Pricing

  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for healthcare organizations
