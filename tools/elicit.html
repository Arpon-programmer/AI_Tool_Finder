Elicit

Description

Elicit is an AI research assistant that helps automate literature review, extract key information from papers, and generate summaries for researchers. It uses language models to find relevant papers and answer research questions efficiently.

Company & History

Elicit is developed by Ought, a non-profit research lab focused on building tools for reasoning and research. Elicit has become popular among academics and scientists for streamlining the research process.

Key Features

  • Automated literature review
  • Paper summarization and extraction
  • Research question answering
  • Data extraction from scientific papers
  • Reference management

How to Use

  • Go to elicit.com and sign up for free.
  • Enter your research question or topic.
  • Review the list of relevant papers and summaries generated by Elicit.
  • Extract data and manage references as needed.

Pricing

  • Free to use
