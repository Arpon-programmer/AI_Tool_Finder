Jasper

Description

Jasper is an AI-powered writing assistant designed to help individuals and businesses create high-quality content quickly. It generates blog posts, marketing copy, social media content, and more using advanced natural language processing.

Company & History

Jasper (formerly Jarvis) was founded in 2021 and quickly became a leader in AI content generation. The company focuses on empowering creators and marketers with tools for fast, effective, and on-brand writing.

Versions & Plans

  • Creator: For individuals and freelancers.
  • Teams: Collaboration, brand voice, and workflow tools.
  • Business: Advanced features, analytics, and support.

How to Use

  • Sign up at jasper.ai.
  • Choose a template or start from scratch.
  • Enter your topic, keywords, and preferences.
  • Generate, edit, and export your content.

Key Features

  • AI-powered content generation
  • Brand voice and style matching
  • Collaboration and workflow tools
  • SEO and marketing integrations
  • Plagiarism checker

Pricing

  • Creator: From $39/month
  • Teams: From $99/month
  • Business: Custom pricing
