Botkeeper

Description

Botkeeper is an AI-powered bookkeeping platform for accounting firms and businesses. It automates data entry, transaction categorization, and financial reporting using machine learning and human oversight.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, Botkeeper combines AI and skilled accountants to deliver scalable bookkeeping solutions for firms and businesses of all sizes.

Versions & Plans

  • Essentials: Core bookkeeping automation.
  • Growth: Advanced analytics and integrations.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large firms.

How to Use

  • Request a demo at botkeeper.com.
  • Connect your financial accounts and software.
  • Let Botkeeper automate bookkeeping tasks.
  • Access reports and insights from the dashboard.

Key Features

  • AI-driven bookkeeping automation
  • Transaction categorization
  • Financial reporting and analytics
  • Human-assisted oversight

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on business needs
