ChemAI

Description

ChemAI is an AI-powered platform for chemical data analysis, molecular property prediction, and research automation in chemistry and materials science.

Company & History

ChemAI is developed by a team of chemists and data scientists to accelerate chemical research and discovery using machine learning and big data.

Versions & Plans

  • Academic: Free or discounted for academic use.
  • Enterprise: Advanced features and support for organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at chemai.com.
  • Upload chemical datasets or input molecular structures.
  • Use AI tools for property prediction and analysis.
  • Download results and reports.

Key Features

  • AI-driven chemical data analysis
  • Molecular property prediction
  • Automated research workflows
  • Data visualization tools

Pricing

  • Academic: Free or discounted
  • Enterprise: Contact for pricing
