Petriage AI

Description

Petriage AI is an AI-powered pet triage and telehealth platform that helps pet owners and veterinarians assess the urgency of pet symptoms and connect with veterinary care. It provides instant guidance and supports better clinical decisions.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, Petriage is trusted by veterinary clinics and pet owners for its clinically validated triage algorithms and telehealth solutions.

Key Features

  • AI-driven pet symptom checker
  • Telehealth consultations with veterinarians
  • Integration with veterinary practice systems
  • Personalized care recommendations
  • 24/7 access for pet owners

How to Use

  • Sign up at petriage.com or download the app.
  • Enter your pet's symptoms for instant triage results.
  • Connect with a veterinarian for further care if needed.

Pricing

  • Free: Basic symptom checker for pet owners
  • Premium: Telehealth and advanced features (subscription)
  • Clinic: Custom pricing for veterinary practices
