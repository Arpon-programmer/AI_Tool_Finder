OCLC Wise AI

Description

OCLC Wise AI is an integrated library system that uses artificial intelligence to personalize library services, automate workflows, and enhance community engagement for public libraries.

Company & History

OCLC is a global library cooperative known for innovative library technologies. Wise is their community engagement system, now enhanced with AI for smarter, more adaptive library services.

Versions & Plans

  • Institutional: Subscription for public libraries and consortia
  • Custom: Tailored solutions for large library systems

How to Use

  • Contact OCLC for a demo or subscription.
  • Integrate Wise AI with your library's existing systems.
  • Leverage AI features for personalized recommendations and workflow automation.

Key Features

  • Personalized patron engagement
  • Automated workflows and notifications
  • Community analytics and insights
  • Integration with library and community platforms

Pricing

  • Institutional subscription (custom pricing)
