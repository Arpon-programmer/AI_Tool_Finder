Outreach AI

Description

Outreach AI is a sales engagement platform that leverages artificial intelligence to optimize sales workflows, automate outreach, and provide actionable insights for sales teams. It helps increase productivity and close more deals.

Company & History

Founded in 2014, Outreach is based in Seattle and is a leader in sales engagement and automation technology for modern sales organizations.

Key Features

  • AI-driven sales automation
  • Predictive analytics and forecasting
  • Multi-channel outreach (email, calls, social)
  • Sales workflow automation
  • CRM integration and reporting

How to Use

  • Request a demo or sign up at outreach.io.
  • Connect your CRM and communication tools.
  • Automate and track sales outreach campaigns.
  • Use AI insights to optimize sales performance.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on organization needs
