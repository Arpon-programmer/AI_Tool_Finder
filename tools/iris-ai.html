Iris.ai

Description

Iris.ai is an AI-powered research platform that helps researchers find, map, and understand scientific knowledge. It automates literature review, concept mapping, and document summarization for academic and corporate research.

Company & History

Iris.ai was founded in 2015 and is based in Norway. It is used by universities, research institutions, and R&D teams worldwide to accelerate scientific discovery.

Key Features

  • AI-driven literature review
  • Concept mapping and knowledge extraction
  • Automated document summarization
  • Research collaboration tools
  • Semantic search across scientific papers

How to Use

  • Sign up at iris.ai.
  • Enter your research topic or upload documents.
  • Explore concept maps and summaries generated by Iris.ai.
  • Collaborate with team members on research projects.

Pricing

  • Free tier available
  • Paid plans for advanced features and teams
