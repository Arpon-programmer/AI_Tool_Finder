D-ID

Description

D-ID is an AI-powered platform for creating talking avatars and video animations from photos and text. It enables animators, marketers, and educators to generate lifelike video content using advanced deep learning models.

Company & History

D-ID was founded in 2017 in Israel. It is trusted by global brands and creators for innovative AI-driven video and avatar generation.

Key Features

  • AI-generated talking avatars from photos
  • Text-to-video and image-to-video animation
  • API for programmatic video creation
  • Integration with creative tools
  • Cloud-based processing

How to Use

  • Sign up at d-id.com.
  • Upload a photo or enter text for your avatar.
  • Use AI to generate talking video content.
  • Download or share your video.

Pricing

  • Free trial available
  • Subscription-based plans
