Quillbot

Description

Quillbot is an AI-powered paraphrasing and summarization tool that helps users rewrite, improve, and summarize text. It is widely used by students, writers, and professionals to enhance clarity, avoid plagiarism, and generate concise summaries.

Company & History

Founded in 2017, Quillbot has become a leading AI writing assistant, serving millions of users worldwide. It offers a suite of tools for paraphrasing, grammar checking, summarizing, and citation generation.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic paraphrasing and summarization features.
  • Premium: Advanced modes, faster processing, and more words per run.

How to Use

  • Go to quillbot.com.
  • Paste or type your text into the input box.
  • Select a mode (Standard, Fluency, Formal, etc.) and click Paraphrase or Summarize.
  • Review and copy the AI-generated output.

Key Features

  • AI-powered paraphrasing and rewriting
  • Summarization and citation tools
  • Grammar and plagiarism checker
  • Multiple writing modes
  • Browser extensions and integrations

Pricing

  • Free: Limited features
  • Premium: $9.95/month or $49.95/year
