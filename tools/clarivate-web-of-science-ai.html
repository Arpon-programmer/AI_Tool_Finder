Clarivate Web of Science AI

Description

Clarivate Web of Science AI leverages artificial intelligence to enhance research discovery, citation analysis, and literature review for academic and scientific communities.

Company & History

Clarivate is a global leader in research analytics and intellectual property. Web of Science is their flagship research platform, now enhanced with AI for smarter insights and recommendations.

Versions & Plans

  • Institutional: Subscription for universities and research organizations
  • Individual: Limited access via personal accounts

How to Use

  • Access via your institution or sign up for an individual account.
  • Use AI-powered search, citation analysis, and research recommendations.
  • Export data and integrate with research management tools.

Key Features

  • AI-driven literature discovery
  • Citation analysis and impact metrics
  • Research trend identification
  • Integration with research management platforms

Pricing

  • Institutional subscription (custom pricing)
