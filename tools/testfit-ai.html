TestFit AI

Description

TestFit AI is a real estate feasibility and building design platform that uses AI to automate site planning, unit mix, parking, and massing studies. It helps architects and developers quickly test and optimize building layouts for multifamily, industrial, and other projects.

Company & History

TestFit was founded in 2017 in the United States. It is widely used by architects, developers, and urban planners for rapid prototyping and feasibility analysis.

Key Features

  • AI-driven site and building layout generation
  • Real-time feasibility and cost analysis
  • Parking and unit mix optimization
  • Integration with CAD and BIM tools
  • Collaboration and reporting features

How to Use

  • Request a demo or sign up at testfit.io.
  • Upload your site plan or define site parameters.
  • Use AI to generate and compare design options.
  • Export results to CAD/BIM or share with stakeholders.
