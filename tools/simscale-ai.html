SimScale AI

Description

SimScale AI brings artificial intelligence to cloud-based engineering simulation, enabling faster, more accurate design iterations and predictive analytics for product development teams.

Company & History

SimScale, founded in 2012, is a pioneer in cloud-native simulation. SimScale AI leverages machine learning to enhance simulation workflows and design optimization.

Versions & Plans

  • Community: Free, public projects only.
  • Professional: Private projects, advanced features.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions and support.

How to Use

  • Sign up at simscale.com.
  • Upload or create your model.
  • Run AI-powered simulations and review results.
  • Iterate and optimize your design.

Key Features

  • AI-driven simulation and optimization
  • Cloud-based collaboration
  • Predictive analytics for engineering
  • Automated design recommendations

Pricing

  • Community: Free
  • Professional: From $250/month
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
