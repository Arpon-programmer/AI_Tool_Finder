Otter.ai

Description

Otter.ai is an AI-powered transcription and note-taking tool that automatically converts speech to text in real time. It is widely used for meetings, lectures, interviews, and collaborative work, providing searchable and shareable transcripts.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, Otter.ai has become a leader in AI transcription, serving millions of users and organizations worldwide. It integrates with popular meeting platforms and offers advanced collaboration features.

Versions & Plans

  • Basic: Free with limited transcription minutes.
  • Pro: More minutes, advanced export, and collaboration features.
  • Business: Team management and integrations.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at otter.ai.
  • Upload audio or join meetings to record and transcribe.
  • Edit, highlight, and share transcripts with your team.
  • Integrate with Zoom, Google Meet, and other platforms.

Key Features

  • Real-time transcription and speaker identification
  • Searchable, editable, and shareable transcripts
  • Collaboration and commenting tools
  • Integrations with meeting platforms
  • Mobile and web apps

Pricing

  • Basic: Free
  • Pro: $16.99/month
  • Business: $30/month per user
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
