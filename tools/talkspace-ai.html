Talkspace AI

Description

Talkspace AI leverages artificial intelligence to enhance online therapy, providing users with mental health support, self-care tools, and personalized insights.

Company & History

Talkspace is a leading online therapy provider founded in 2012. The company integrates AI to support therapists and users, making mental health care more accessible and data-driven.

Versions & Plans

  • Subscription: Weekly and monthly plans for individuals
  • Business: Employee assistance and enterprise solutions

How to Use

  • Sign up at talkspace.com.
  • Choose a plan and get matched with a licensed therapist.
  • Use AI-powered tools for self-care and progress tracking.

Key Features

  • AI-powered therapy support
  • Personalized self-care recommendations
  • Progress tracking and insights
  • Secure and confidential platform

Pricing

  • Subscription-based plans
