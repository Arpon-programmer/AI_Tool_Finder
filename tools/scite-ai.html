Scite.ai

Description

Scite.ai is an AI-powered platform that helps researchers evaluate the reliability and context of scientific citations. It shows how a scientific paper has been cited—whether supporting, mentioning, or contrasting—across the literature.

Company & History

Scite.ai was founded in 2018 and is based in New York. It is widely used by researchers, publishers, and institutions to assess the impact and credibility of scientific work.

Key Features

  • Smart citation analysis
  • Contextual citation statements
  • Paper reliability and impact metrics
  • Browser extension for citation insights
  • Integrations with reference managers

How to Use

  • Go to scite.ai and create an account.
  • Search for scientific papers or authors.
  • View citation contexts and reliability metrics.
  • Use the browser extension for citation analysis while browsing.

Pricing

  • Free tier available
  • Paid plans for advanced analytics and integrations
