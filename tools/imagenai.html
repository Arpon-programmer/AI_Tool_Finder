ImagenAI

Description

ImagenAI is an AI-powered photo editing assistant that learns your unique editing style and automatically edits large batches of photos, saving time for professional photographers and studios.

Company & History

ImagenAI was founded in 2020 and is based in Israel. It is designed to help photographers streamline their workflow and maintain a consistent editing style across projects.

Key Features

  • Personalized AI photo editing
  • Batch processing for large photo sets
  • Style learning and adaptation
  • Integration with Adobe Lightroom
  • Cloud-based workflow

How to Use

  • Sign up at imagen-ai.com.
  • Upload your Lightroom catalog and let ImagenAI learn your style.
  • Batch edit photos automatically and review results.

Pricing

  • Pay-per-photo pricing
  • Free trial available
