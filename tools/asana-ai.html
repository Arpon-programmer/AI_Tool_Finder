Asana AI

Description

Asana AI brings artificial intelligence and automation to the Asana work management platform, helping teams automate tasks, predict project risks, and optimize workflows for better productivity.

Company & History

Asana, founded in 2008 and based in San Francisco, is a leading provider of work management software. Its AI features help teams manage projects more efficiently.

Key Features

  • AI-powered task automation
  • Project risk prediction
  • Smart project templates
  • Workflow optimization
  • Integration with other productivity tools

How to Use

  • Enable AI features in your Asana workspace at asana.com/ai.
  • Configure automation rules and smart templates.
  • Monitor project progress and leverage AI insights.

Pricing

  • Included in select Asana plans
  • Additional costs for advanced AI features
