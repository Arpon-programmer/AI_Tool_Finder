AlphaFold

Description

AlphaFold is an AI system developed by DeepMind that predicts protein structures with high accuracy, revolutionizing biological and medical research.

Company & History

AlphaFold was created by DeepMind, a subsidiary of Alphabet, and released in 2021. It has been recognized as a breakthrough in computational biology.

Versions & Plans

  • Open Source: Free and available to the scientific community.
  • AlphaFold Database: Public access to predicted protein structures.

How to Use

  • Access the AlphaFold Protein Structure Database at alphafold.ebi.ac.uk.
  • Search for protein structures or download predictions.
  • Use open-source code for custom predictions.

Key Features

  • Accurate protein structure prediction
  • Open-source and freely available
  • Extensive protein database
  • Widely used in research and drug discovery

Pricing

  • Open Source: Free
