Paradox AI

Description

Paradox AI is an HR automation platform featuring Olivia, an AI assistant that automates candidate screening, interview scheduling, and communication to streamline recruiting processes.

Company & History

Paradox, founded in 2016, is known for its conversational AI solutions in talent acquisition, serving clients worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Standard: Core automation and AI assistant features.
  • Enterprise: Advanced integrations and analytics.

How to Use

  • Set up Paradox AI for your recruiting workflow.
  • Automate candidate screening and scheduling.
  • Use analytics to optimize hiring processes.

Key Features

  • Conversational AI assistant (Olivia)
  • Automated scheduling and screening
  • Integration with HR and ATS systems
  • Analytics and reporting

Pricing

  • Standard: Custom pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
