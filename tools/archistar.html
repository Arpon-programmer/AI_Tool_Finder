Archistar

Description

Archistar is an AI-powered property and site analysis platform for architects, developers, and planners. It automates site finding, feasibility studies, and generative design to accelerate property development and maximize site potential.

Company & History

Archistar was founded in 2011 in Australia. It is trusted by leading property professionals for fast, data-driven site analysis and design.

Key Features

  • AI-driven site finding and feasibility analysis
  • Automated generative design
  • Planning and zoning compliance checks
  • 3D visualization and reporting
  • Integration with CAD/BIM tools

How to Use

  • Sign up at archistar.ai.
  • Search for sites or upload your own.
  • Run AI-powered feasibility and design studies.
  • Export results or share with your team.
