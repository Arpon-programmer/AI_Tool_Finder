DoNotPay

Description

DoNotPay is an AI-powered legal chatbot and consumer rights platform. It helps users contest parking tickets, cancel subscriptions, draft legal documents, and resolve various legal issues through automated workflows.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, DoNotPay started as a chatbot for fighting parking tickets and has expanded to cover a wide range of legal and consumer services.

Versions & Plans

  • Subscription: Monthly plan for unlimited access to services.

How to Use

  • Sign up at donotpay.com.
  • Choose a service (e.g., contest ticket, cancel subscription).
  • Follow the chatbot prompts to complete your request.
  • Download or send generated documents as needed.

Key Features

  • AI legal chatbot
  • Document generation
  • Consumer rights advocacy
  • Automated legal workflows

Pricing

  • Subscription: $36 every 2 months
