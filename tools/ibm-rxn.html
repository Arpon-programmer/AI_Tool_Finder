IBM RXN for Chemistry

Description

IBM RXN for Chemistry is an AI-powered platform for predicting chemical reactions, retrosynthesis, and molecular design, accelerating research in chemistry and drug discovery.

Company & History

Developed by IBM Research, RXN for Chemistry leverages deep learning to transform chemical synthesis and was launched as a cloud platform in 2018.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic access for academic and personal use.
  • Enterprise: Advanced features and integrations for organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at rxn.res.ibm.com.
  • Input reactants or target molecules.
  • Use AI to predict reactions or retrosynthesis pathways.
  • Export results for further analysis.

Key Features

  • AI-powered reaction prediction
  • Retrosynthesis planning
  • Molecular design tools
  • Cloud-based and accessible

Pricing

  • Free: Basic access
  • Enterprise: Contact IBM for pricing
