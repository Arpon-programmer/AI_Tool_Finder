Fitbod AI

Description

Fitbod AI is a smart workout planner that uses artificial intelligence to generate personalized strength training routines based on user goals, equipment, and progress.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, Fitbod leverages AI and data analytics to help users optimize their fitness routines and achieve better results in the gym or at home.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited workouts and features.
  • Elite: Full access to AI workout plans and analytics (subscription).

How to Use

  • Download the Fitbod app from fitbod.me.
  • Set your fitness goals, available equipment, and preferences.
  • Follow AI-generated workout routines and track your progress.

Key Features

  • AI-powered workout generation
  • Personalized strength training plans
  • Progress tracking and analytics
  • Integration with health and fitness apps

Pricing

  • Free: Limited features
  • Elite: Subscription required
