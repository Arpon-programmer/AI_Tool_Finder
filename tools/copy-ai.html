Copy.ai

Description

Copy.ai is an AI-powered copywriting tool that helps users generate marketing copy, blog content, emails, and more. It leverages advanced language models to create engaging and effective text for businesses and individuals.

Company & History

Founded in 2020, Copy.ai quickly became a leader in AI content generation. The company focuses on making high-quality copy accessible to everyone, from startups to enterprises, with a user-friendly platform and continuous feature updates.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited credits and features.
  • Pro: Unlimited words, advanced tools, and priority support.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large teams and organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at copy.ai.
  • Choose a content type or template.
  • Enter your topic, keywords, and preferences.
  • Generate, edit, and export your copy.

Key Features

  • AI-powered copywriting for multiple formats
  • Blog, ad, email, and social media content
  • Team collaboration tools
  • Brand voice customization
  • Integrations and API

Pricing

  • Free: Limited usage
  • Pro: From $49/month
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
