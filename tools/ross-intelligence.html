ROSS Intelligence

Description

ROSS Intelligence was an AI-powered legal research tool that used natural language processing to help lawyers find relevant case law and legal information quickly. It was known for its user-friendly interface and advanced search capabilities.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, ROSS Intelligence was a pioneer in AI legal research. The company ceased operations in 2021 but had a significant impact on legal technology.

Versions & Plans

  • Discontinued: No longer available as of 2021.

How to Use

  • Service discontinued. Previously, users could enter legal questions and receive AI-powered research results.

Key Features

  • AI-powered legal research
  • Natural language search
  • Case law analysis
  • User-friendly interface

Pricing

  • Service discontinued
