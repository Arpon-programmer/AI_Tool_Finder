Totango

Description

Totango is an AI-driven customer health scoring and engagement platform, enabling customer success teams to proactively manage accounts, reduce churn, and drive growth.

Company & History

Founded in 2010, Totango is a leading provider of customer success software for SaaS and enterprise businesses.

Key Features

  • AI-powered customer health scoring
  • Automated engagement workflows
  • Churn prediction and alerts
  • Customer journey mapping
  • CRM and support tool integrations

How to Use

  • Request a demo at totango.com.
  • Integrate with your CRM and customer data.
  • Monitor customer health and automate engagement.

Pricing
