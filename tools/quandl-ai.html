Quandl AI

Description

Quandl AI provides AI-powered economic and financial data analytics, enabling users to access, analyze, and visualize global financial datasets.

Company & History

Founded in 2011 and acquired by Nasdaq in 2018, Quandl is a leading provider of financial, economic, and alternative data for investment professionals.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited access to select datasets.
  • Premium: Full access to premium and alternative data feeds.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions and integrations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at quandl.com.
  • Browse or search for datasets.
  • Use the API or web interface to access and analyze data.
  • Download or integrate data into your workflow.

Key Features

  • AI-powered data analytics
  • Extensive financial and economic datasets
  • API and integration support
  • Custom data solutions

Pricing

  • Free: Limited access
  • Premium: Contact for pricing
  • Enterprise: Contact for pricing
