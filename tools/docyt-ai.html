Docyt AI

Description

Docyt AI is an AI-powered financial automation platform for small and medium businesses. It automates bookkeeping, expense management, document processing, and provides real-time financial insights.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, Docyt leverages AI to help businesses streamline financial operations, reduce manual work, and improve accuracy in accounting.

Versions & Plans

  • Starter: Basic automation and document management.
  • Professional: Advanced automation and integrations.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for larger businesses.

How to Use

  • Sign up at docyt.com.
  • Connect your financial accounts and upload documents.
  • Let Docyt AI automate bookkeeping and expense tracking.
  • Access real-time reports and insights.

Key Features

  • AI-powered bookkeeping
  • Expense and document management
  • Real-time financial dashboards
  • Integrations with accounting software

Pricing

  • Starter: $99/month
  • Professional: $199/month
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
