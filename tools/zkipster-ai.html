Zkipster AI

Description

Zkipster AI is an event guest list and check-in platform that uses AI to streamline guest management, invitations, and event analytics for professional events.

Company & History

Founded in 2009, Zkipster is trusted by leading brands and event professionals for secure, efficient, and data-driven event guest management.

Versions & Plans

  • Essential: Core guest list and check-in features.
  • Professional: Advanced guest management and analytics.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at zkipster.com.
  • Import or create guest lists and invitations.
  • Use AI tools for check-in, guest tracking, and analytics.
  • Export reports and collaborate with your team.

Key Features

  • AI-powered guest list management
  • Event check-in and tracking
  • Invitation and RSVP automation
  • Analytics and reporting
