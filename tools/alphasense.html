AlphaSense

Description

AlphaSense is an AI-powered market intelligence and search platform designed for business professionals, researchers, and analysts. It enables users to search, monitor, and analyze financial documents, earnings calls, news, and research reports to make informed decisions faster.

Company & History

Founded in 2011, AlphaSense is headquartered in New York and has become a leading provider of AI-driven search and market intelligence solutions for enterprises, financial institutions, and consultancies worldwide.

Key Features

  • AI-powered semantic search across millions of documents
  • Real-time alerts and monitoring for market-moving events
  • Access to broker research, SEC filings, news, and transcripts
  • Collaboration tools for teams
  • Advanced analytics and trend detection

How to Use

  • Sign up for an AlphaSense account at alpha-sense.com.
  • Use the search bar to find relevant documents, news, and research.
  • Set up alerts to monitor topics, companies, or industries.
  • Leverage collaboration features to share insights with your team.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on organization size and needs
  • Free trial available upon request
