Ex Libris Alma AI

Description

Ex Libris Alma AI enhances library management with artificial intelligence, automating workflows, optimizing collections, and providing advanced analytics for academic and research libraries.

Company & History

Ex Libris, part of Clarivate, is a global leader in library automation. Alma is their unified library services platform, and its AI features help libraries operate more efficiently and deliver better services.

Versions & Plans

  • Institutional: Subscription for academic and research libraries
  • Consortial: Multi-library and networked solutions

How to Use

  • Contact Ex Libris for a demo or institutional subscription.
  • Integrate with your library's workflows and data.
  • Use AI-driven analytics and automation features in Alma.

Key Features

  • Automated workflows and task management
  • AI-powered analytics and reporting
  • Collection optimization
  • Integration with library discovery tools

Pricing

  • Institutional subscription (custom pricing)
