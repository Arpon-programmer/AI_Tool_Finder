Paxata

Description

Paxata is an AI-driven data integration and quality management platform that empowers data engineers to prepare, clean, and enrich data for analytics and business intelligence.

Company & History

Founded in 2012 and acquired by DataRobot in 2019, Paxata is known for its self-service data preparation and smart data enrichment capabilities.

Key Features

  • AI-powered data integration and cleaning
  • Smart data enrichment and profiling
  • Collaboration and workflow automation
  • Integration with BI and analytics tools
  • Data quality monitoring

How to Use

  • Request a demo at paxata.com.
  • Connect your data sources and start preparing data.
  • Use AI-driven tools to clean, enrich, and export data.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing for enterprise clients
