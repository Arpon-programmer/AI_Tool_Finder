Notion AI

Description

Notion AI is an integrated artificial intelligence assistant within Notion, designed to help users write, brainstorm, summarize, and automate tasks in their notes, docs, and wikis. It enhances productivity and creativity for individuals and teams.

Company & History

Notion Labs Inc., founded in 2013, is a leading productivity software company. Notion AI was launched in 2023, bringing generative AI and automation to millions of Notion users worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited AI responses per month.
  • Plus: More AI responses, advanced features, and priority support.
  • Business/Enterprise: Admin controls, security, and unlimited AI usage.

How to Use

  • Sign up at notion.so.
  • Open any Notion page and click the AI icon or use the AI command.
  • Ask questions, generate content, or automate tasks with AI.
  • Upgrade for more advanced AI features and higher usage limits.

Key Features

  • AI-powered writing, brainstorming, and summarization
  • Task automation and workflow suggestions
  • Integrated with Notion docs, wikis, and databases
  • Supports multiple languages
  • Custom AI automations (Plus/Business)

Pricing

  • Free: Limited AI responses
  • Plus: $8/month per user
  • Business: $15/month per user
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
