Nutrino AI

Description

Nutrino AI is an AI-powered nutrition insights and meal planning platform that provides personalized dietary recommendations based on health data and preferences.

Company & History

Founded in 2014 and acquired by Medtronic, Nutrino leverages AI and data science to help users and healthcare professionals make better nutrition decisions.

Versions & Plans

  • Consumer: Personalized nutrition app for individuals.
  • Professional: Solutions for healthcare providers and organizations.

How to Use

  • Visit nutrino.co.
  • Input health data and dietary preferences.
  • Receive personalized meal plans and nutrition insights.

Key Features

  • AI-powered nutrition analysis
  • Personalized meal planning
  • Integration with health devices
  • Data-driven dietary recommendations
