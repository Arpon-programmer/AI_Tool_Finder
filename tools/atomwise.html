Atomwise

Description

Atomwise uses AI for structure-based drug discovery, enabling biomedical engineers to identify promising compounds and accelerate pharmaceutical research.

Company & History

Atomwise is a San Francisco-based biotech company pioneering AI in drug discovery since 2012, with a focus on small molecule therapeutics.

Key Features

  • AI-powered molecular screening
  • Structure-based drug design
  • Automated compound selection
  • Integration with biomedical research workflows
  • Collaborative research partnerships

How to Use

  • Contact Atomwise for research collaboration at atomwise.com.
  • Submit research proposals for AI-driven drug discovery.
  • Leverage AI models for compound screening and selection.

Pricing
