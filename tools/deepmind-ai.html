DeepMind AI

Description

DeepMind AI develops advanced artificial intelligence for scientific discovery, including breakthroughs in physics, biology, and mathematics.

Company & History

Founded in 2010 and acquired by Google in 2014, DeepMind is a world leader in AI research, known for AlphaGo, AlphaFold, and other scientific AI systems.
