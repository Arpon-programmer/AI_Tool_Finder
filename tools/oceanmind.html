OceanMind

Description

OceanMind uses AI and satellite data to monitor, analyze, and protect global fisheries and marine environments, supporting sustainable ocean management.

Company & History

Founded in 2017, OceanMind is a UK-based non-profit organization dedicated to ocean conservation and compliance monitoring using advanced technology.

Versions & Plans

  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for governments, NGOs, and industry partners.

How to Use

  • Contact OceanMind via oceanmind.global for partnership or service inquiries.

  • Leverage AI insights for sustainable fisheries management.
