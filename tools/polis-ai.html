Polis AI

Description

Polis AI is a public opinion analysis platform that uses artificial intelligence to gather, analyze, and visualize feedback for policy development and civic engagement.

Company & History

Polis is an open-source project used by governments and organizations worldwide to facilitate large-scale, AI-driven public discussions and policy feedback.

Versions & Plans

  • Open Source: Free for public use
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for organizations

How to Use

  • Access Polis online or deploy your own instance.
  • Set up a conversation and invite participants.
  • Use AI analytics to interpret public feedback.

Key Features

  • AI-powered opinion clustering
  • Real-time visualization of group consensus
  • Open-source and privacy-focused
  • Used in government and civic tech

Pricing

  • Open source (free) and enterprise pricing
