Flexport AI

Description

Flexport AI leverages artificial intelligence to optimize global logistics, automate supply chain workflows, and provide predictive analytics for freight, inventory, and shipping management.

Company & History

Flexport, founded in 2013, is a leading digital freight forwarder and supply chain platform. Flexport AI is part of their ongoing innovation to bring automation and intelligence to logistics and supply chain management worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Standard: Core logistics and supply chain AI features.
  • Enterprise: Advanced analytics, integrations, and custom solutions.

How to Use

  • Sign up at flexport.com.
  • Connect your supply chain data and configure workflows.
  • Use AI-powered dashboards for shipment tracking, demand forecasting, and inventory optimization.
  • Integrate with ERP, WMS, and other business systems as needed.

Key Features

  • AI-driven freight and inventory optimization
  • Predictive analytics for supply chain disruptions
  • Automated shipment tracking and notifications
  • Customizable dashboards and reporting
  • Integration with major business platforms

Pricing

  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
