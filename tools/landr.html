LANDR

Description

LANDR is an AI-powered mastering and music distribution platform that helps music producers finalize tracks with professional quality and distribute them to major streaming services.

Company & History

Founded in 2012, LANDR is a leader in AI audio mastering and music technology, serving millions of artists worldwide.

Key Features

  • AI audio mastering
  • Music distribution to streaming platforms
  • Collaboration and project management
  • Sample library and plugins
  • Royalty management

How to Use

  • Sign up at landr.com.
  • Upload your track for AI mastering.
  • Distribute your music to streaming services.

Pricing

  • Subscription and pay-per-use options
