Description

X2AI provides AI-driven mental health support, offering personalized, scalable, and confidential conversations for individuals and organizations.

Company & History

X2AI (X2) is a company focused on delivering scalable, culturally sensitive mental health support using AI. Their solutions are used by organizations, clinics, and individuals worldwide.

  • Conversations are confidential and available 24/7.

  • Data privacy and security
