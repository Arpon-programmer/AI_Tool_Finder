Monday.com AI

Description

Monday.com AI brings artificial intelligence and automation to the Monday.com work operating system, helping teams automate workflows, predict project risks, and optimize productivity across projects.

Company & History

Monday.com, founded in 2012 and based in Tel Aviv, is a leading work management platform. Its AI features help teams streamline processes and make data-driven decisions.

Key Features

  • AI-powered workflow automation
  • Project risk prediction
  • Smart notifications and reminders
  • Integration with third-party tools
  • Custom automation recipes

How to Use

  • Enable AI features in your Monday.com workspace at monday.com/ai.
  • Set up automation recipes and smart notifications.
  • Monitor project progress and leverage AI insights.

Pricing

  • Included in select Monday.com plans
  • Additional costs for advanced AI features
