Signal AI

Description

Signal AI is an AI-powered media monitoring and reputation management platform for PR professionals, providing real-time insights and analytics from global news and social media.

Company & History

Signal AI is a pioneer in AI-driven media intelligence, helping organizations manage brand reputation and monitor media coverage worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Professional: Subscription for PR teams
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large organizations

How to Use

  • Sign up for Signal AI and set up your monitoring topics.
  • Track media mentions and sentiment in real time.
  • Use AI analytics for reputation management and reporting.

Key Features

  • AI-powered media monitoring
  • Reputation analytics
  • Real-time alerts
  • Customizable dashboards

Pricing

  • Professional and enterprise subscriptions
