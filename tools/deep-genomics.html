Deep Genomics uses AI to discover and develop genetic medicines, accelerating drug discovery and improving outcomes for biomedical engineers and researchers.

Deep Genomics is a biotechnology company pioneering the use of AI in genomics and drug development, founded in Toronto, Canada.
