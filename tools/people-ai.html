People.ai

Description

People.ai is an AI-powered revenue operations and intelligence platform that automates data capture, delivers actionable insights, and helps sales teams drive growth. It connects sales activity data to outcomes for better forecasting and productivity.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, People.ai is based in San Francisco and serves enterprise sales teams with advanced AI-driven analytics and automation.

Key Features

  • Automated sales activity capture
  • Revenue intelligence and analytics
  • Pipeline management and forecasting
  • Integration with CRM and sales tools
  • AI-driven recommendations for sales teams

How to Use

  • Request a demo or sign up at people.ai.
  • Connect your CRM and sales tools.
  • Automate data capture and analyze sales performance.
  • Use insights to improve pipeline and revenue outcomes.

Pricing
