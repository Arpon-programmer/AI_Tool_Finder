MedWhat

Description

MedWhat is an AI-powered medical and drug information assistant that answers health and medication questions, helping pharmacists, clinicians, and patients with evidence-based information in real time.

Company & History

Founded in 2012, MedWhat is a pioneer in medical AI chatbots, serving healthcare professionals and consumers globally with advanced natural language processing technology.

Key Features

  • AI-powered medical and drug Q&A
  • Evidence-based health information
  • Integration with healthcare systems and apps
  • 24/7 conversational support
  • Multilingual capabilities

How to Use

  • Access MedWhat via web or partner platforms.
  • Type your medical or drug question for instant answers.
  • Integrate MedWhat API for clinical or pharmacy use.

Pricing

  • Free: Basic Q&A for consumers
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for healthcare organizations
