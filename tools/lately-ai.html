Lately AI

Description

Lately AI is an AI-powered social media content generator and management platform. It uses natural language processing to repurpose long-form content into dozens of social posts, helping brands and marketers scale their social media presence.

Company & History

Lately AI was founded in 2014 in the United States. It is trusted by global brands for automating and optimizing social media content creation.

Key Features

  • AI-driven content repurposing
  • Multi-channel social media scheduling
  • Analytics and performance tracking
  • Collaboration tools for teams
  • Integrations with major social platforms

How to Use

  • Sign up at lately.ai.
  • Upload long-form content (blogs, videos, etc.).
  • Let the AI generate social posts and schedule them.
  • Track performance and optimize your strategy.
