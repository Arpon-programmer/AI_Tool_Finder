Ada Support

Description

Ada Support is an AI-powered customer service automation platform that enables businesses to deliver instant, personalized support at scale. It automates common customer inquiries and integrates with live agents for seamless handoffs.

Company & History

Founded in 2016 and based in Toronto, Ada is trusted by global brands to automate millions of customer interactions every month.

Key Features

  • AI-powered chatbots for customer support
  • Personalized automated responses
  • Seamless handoff to live agents
  • Multilingual support
  • Integrations with CRM and helpdesk tools

How to Use

  • Sign up at ada.cx.
  • Configure your chatbot and automation flows.
  • Integrate with your website or support channels.
  • Monitor and optimize customer interactions.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on business needs
