Google Health AI

Description

Google Health AI is a collection of artificial intelligence solutions and research projects by Google aimed at improving healthcare delivery, diagnostics, and patient outcomes. It includes tools for medical imaging, disease prediction, and clinical workflow optimization.

Company & History

Google Health, a division of Google, collaborates with healthcare providers and researchers worldwide to develop AI models and digital health tools. Their work spans from early disease detection to population health management.

Key Solutions

  • AI-powered medical imaging analysis
  • Predictive analytics for disease risk
  • Clinical documentation and workflow tools
  • Population health and research platforms
