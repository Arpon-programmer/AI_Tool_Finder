DeepMotion

Description

DeepMotion is an AI-powered motion capture and animation platform that transforms video into 3D animations. It enables animators and creators to generate realistic character movements using AI-driven motion analysis.

Company & History

DeepMotion was founded in 2014 in the United States. It is trusted by animators, game developers, and studios for fast, accurate motion capture and animation.

Key Features

  • AI-powered motion capture from video
  • 3D character animation generation
  • Facial and hand tracking
  • Integration with major 3D software
  • Cloud-based processing

How to Use

  • Sign up at deepmotion.com.
  • Upload your video or animation project.
  • Use AI to generate 3D motion data and animations.
  • Download and import results into your workflow.

Pricing

  • Free trial available
  • Subscription-based plans
