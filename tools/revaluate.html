Revaluate

Description

Revaluate is an AI platform for predicting real estate leads most likely to move, enabling agents to target prospects with high conversion potential.

Company & History

Founded in 2014, Revaluate is a leader in predictive analytics for real estate marketing and lead generation.

Key Features

  • AI-driven lead scoring and prediction
  • Data enrichment and segmentation
  • Automated marketing campaigns
  • CRM integration
  • Performance analytics

How to Use

  • Sign up at revaluate.com.
  • Upload or sync your contact database.
  • Target and engage high-potential leads.

Pricing

  • Subscription-based pricing
