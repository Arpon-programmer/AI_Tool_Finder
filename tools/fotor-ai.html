Fotor AI

Description

Fotor AI is an online photo editing platform that uses artificial intelligence to enhance, retouch, and generate images. It offers tools for background removal, photo effects, AI art generation, and more for both casual and professional users.

Company & History

Fotor was launched in 2012 and has evolved into a leading AI-powered photo editor, serving millions of users worldwide with both web and mobile apps.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic editing and limited AI features.
  • Pro: Advanced AI tools and higher resolution exports.
  • Pro+: All features, priority support, and commercial use.

How to Use

  • Go to fotor.com.
  • Upload your photo or use AI art generator.
  • Apply effects, retouch, or generate new images.
  • Download or share your results.

Key Features

  • AI photo enhancement and retouching
  • Background remover and object detection
  • AI art and avatar generator
  • Batch processing
  • Templates and design tools

Pricing

  • Free: Limited features
  • Pro: $8.99/month
  • Pro+: $19.99/month
