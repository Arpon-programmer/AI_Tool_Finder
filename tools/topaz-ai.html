Topaz AI

Description

Topaz AI is a suite of AI-powered photo editing tools by Topaz Labs, specializing in image upscaling, noise reduction, sharpening, and restoration for photographers and creative professionals.

Company & History

Topaz Labs, founded in 2005, is known for its advanced AI-based photo enhancement software, including Topaz Photo AI, Gigapixel AI, DeNoise AI, and Sharpen AI.

Key Features

  • AI image upscaling (Gigapixel AI)
  • Noise reduction (DeNoise AI)
  • Image sharpening (Sharpen AI)
  • Photo restoration and enhancement
  • Batch processing

How to Use

  • Download Topaz AI products from topazlabs.com.
  • Open your images and apply AI-powered enhancements.
  • Export high-quality, enhanced images for print or web.

Pricing

  • One-time purchase per product or bundle
  • Free trial available
