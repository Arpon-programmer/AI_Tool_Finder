Sentieo

Description

Sentieo is an AI-powered financial research platform that combines document search, data analytics, and modeling tools for investment professionals. It streamlines the research process by integrating financial data, filings, and news in one platform.

Company & History

Founded in 2012, Sentieo is headquartered in San Francisco and serves asset managers, investment banks, and corporations with advanced research and analytics solutions.

Key Features

  • AI-powered document search and summarization
  • Financial data integration and visualization
  • Collaboration and note-taking tools
  • Modeling and analytics capabilities
  • Real-time alerts and monitoring

How to Use

  • Request a demo or trial at sentieo.com.
  • Search and analyze financial documents and data.
  • Collaborate with team members and build models.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing for enterprise clients
  • Free trial or demo available
