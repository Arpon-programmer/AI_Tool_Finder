Wildbook AI

Description

Wildbook AI is an open-source platform for wildlife identification and tracking, using AI and computer vision to support conservation and research.

Company & History

Developed by Wild Me, Wildbook AI is used by conservationists and researchers worldwide to monitor animal populations and biodiversity.

Versions & Plans

  • Open Source: Free access for research and conservation projects.

How to Use

  • Visit wildme.org to access Wildbook projects.
  • Upload wildlife images for identification and tracking.
  • Participate in global conservation initiatives.

Key Features

  • AI-powered wildlife identification
  • Open-source and community-driven
  • Support for conservation and research
  • Global wildlife databases

Pricing
