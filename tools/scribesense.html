ScribeSense

Description

ScribeSense is an AI-powered grading and assessment tool for educators. It automates the grading of assignments, quizzes, and tests, saving teachers time and providing detailed analytics on student performance.

Company & History

ScribeSense was founded to help educators streamline the assessment process using artificial intelligence. It is used by schools and institutions to improve grading efficiency and accuracy.

Versions & Plans

  • Basic: For individual teachers and small classrooms.
  • School: For schools and districts with multiple users.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large institutions.

How to Use

  • Sign up at scribesense.com.
  • Upload scanned or digital student assignments.
  • Let the AI grade and analyze the submissions.
  • Review results and export grades to your LMS.

Key Features

  • Automated grading for various assignment types
  • Detailed analytics and reporting
  • Integration with learning management systems
  • Secure data handling
  • Support for handwritten and digital submissions
