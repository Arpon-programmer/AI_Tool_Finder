Yewno Discover

Description

Yewno Discover is an AI-powered research platform that helps users explore academic content, visualize knowledge connections, and uncover new insights across disciplines.

Company & History

Yewno, founded in 2014, specializes in knowledge graph technology and semantic AI for research and education. Yewno Discover is used by universities and libraries worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Institutional: Licensing for universities, libraries, and research organizations
  • Demo: Limited access for evaluation

How to Use

  • Access via your institution or request a demo from Yewno.
  • Search for academic topics and explore knowledge graphs.
  • Use semantic tools to discover new connections and insights.

Key Features

  • Knowledge graph visualization
  • Semantic search and discovery
  • Academic content aggregation
  • Insight generation tools

Pricing

  • Institutional licensing
