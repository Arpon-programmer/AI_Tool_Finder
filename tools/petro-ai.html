Petro.ai

Description

Petro.ai is an AI-powered platform for geological data analysis, oil and gas exploration, and reservoir management, providing advanced analytics for geoscientists and engineers.

Company & History

Founded in 2013, Petro.ai delivers data-driven solutions for the energy sector, focusing on subsurface analytics and digital transformation in oil and gas.
