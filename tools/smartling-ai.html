Smartling AI

Description

Smartling AI is a translation management platform that uses artificial intelligence to automate and optimize the localization process for websites, apps, and content. It combines machine translation, workflow automation, and human review for high-quality results.

Company & History

Smartling was founded in 2009 in New York. It is trusted by global brands to manage and scale their multilingual content efficiently.

Key Features

  • AI-powered translation management
  • Automated workflow and quality checks
  • Integration with CMS and code repositories
  • Real-time collaboration and review
  • Analytics and reporting

How to Use

  • Request a demo or sign up at smartling.com.
  • Connect your content sources and configure workflows.
  • Automate translation and review processes.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing for enterprise clients
