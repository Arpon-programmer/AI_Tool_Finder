Coda AI

Description

Coda AI enhances document collaboration and workflow automation with artificial intelligence, enabling users to generate content, automate tasks, and analyze data within Coda docs.

Company & History

Coda, founded in 2014, is a modern document platform. Its AI features help teams work more efficiently by automating repetitive tasks and generating insights.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Basic docs and AI features.
  • Pro: Advanced AI and automation tools.
  • Enterprise: Custom security and support.

How to Use

  • Sign up at coda.io.
  • Create or import your documents.
  • Enable AI features for content generation and automation.
  • Collaborate and share with your team.

Key Features

  • AI-powered content generation
  • Workflow automation
  • Data analysis and insights
  • Integration with third-party tools

Pricing

  • Free: $0
  • Pro: $12/user/month
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
