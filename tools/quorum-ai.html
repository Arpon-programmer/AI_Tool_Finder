Quorum AI

Description

Quorum AI is a legislative tracking and policy analysis platform that uses artificial intelligence to monitor, analyze, and predict policy trends and outcomes.

Company & History

Quorum is a leading provider of public affairs software, helping policy professionals leverage AI for legislative research and advocacy.

  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large organizations
