Adobe Animate AI

Description

Adobe Animate AI brings artificial intelligence features to Adobe Animate, enabling automated character rigging, smart in-betweening, and motion prediction for animators. It streamlines the animation process and enhances creative workflows.

Company & History

Adobe Animate is a leading animation software by Adobe. Its AI features are powered by Adobe Sensei, helping animators create high-quality animations faster.

Key Features

  • AI-powered character rigging
  • Smart in-betweening and motion prediction
  • Automated lip-sync and facial animation
  • Integration with Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Export to multiple formats

How to Use

  • Subscribe to Adobe Animate at adobe.com.
  • Open your animation project and enable AI features.
  • Use AI tools for rigging, in-betweening, and lip-sync.
  • Export and share your animation.

Pricing

  • Subscription-based pricing
  • Free trial available
