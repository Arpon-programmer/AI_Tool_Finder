Casebook AI

Description

Casebook AI is a cloud-based case management platform that uses artificial intelligence to help social workers manage cases, automate documentation, and improve client outcomes.

Company & History

Casebook PBC is a public benefit corporation focused on providing modern, AI-powered software for human services and social work professionals.

Key Features

  • AI-assisted case notes and documentation
  • Automated workflow management
  • Client progress tracking
  • Data-driven insights and reporting
  • Secure, cloud-based access

How to Use

  • Request a demo or sign up at casebook.net.
  • Set up your organization and client profiles.
  • Use AI features to automate documentation and manage cases.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing for organizations
