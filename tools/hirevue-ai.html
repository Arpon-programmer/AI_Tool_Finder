HireVue AI

Description

HireVue AI leverages artificial intelligence to streamline the hiring process, offering video interviewing, assessments, and automation to help organizations identify top talent efficiently and fairly.

Company & History

HireVue, founded in 2004, is a pioneer in digital interviewing and AI-driven talent assessment, serving global enterprises in their recruitment efforts.

Versions & Plans

  • Standard: Video interviewing and basic AI assessments.
  • Enterprise: Advanced analytics, integrations, and support.

How to Use

  • Sign up and configure your hiring workflow.
  • Invite candidates to complete video interviews and assessments.
  • Review AI-driven insights and shortlist candidates.

Key Features

  • AI-powered video interviewing
  • Automated candidate assessments
  • Bias mitigation tools
  • Integration with ATS platforms

Pricing

  • Standard: Custom pricing
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
