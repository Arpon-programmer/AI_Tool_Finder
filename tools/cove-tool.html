Cove.tool

Description

Cove.tool is an AI-powered platform for sustainable building design and performance analysis. It helps architects and engineers optimize energy, daylight, carbon, and cost for new and existing buildings.

Company & History

Cove.tool was founded in 2017 in Atlanta, USA. It is widely used by AEC professionals to streamline sustainability analysis and meet green building standards.

Key Features

  • AI-driven energy and daylight modeling
  • Carbon and cost optimization
  • Automated code compliance checks
  • Integration with Revit, Rhino, SketchUp, and more
  • Collaboration and reporting tools

How to Use

  • Sign up at covetool.com.
  • Import your building model or create a new project.
  • Run AI-powered analysis for energy, daylight, and carbon.
  • Optimize design and export reports for compliance.

Pricing

  • Subscription-based pricing
  • Free trial available
