CityFormLab AI

Description

CityFormLab AI is a research-driven platform for urban data analysis, simulation, and visualization, supporting innovative urban planning and design.

Company & History

CityFormLab is a research group at MIT focused on urban analytics, computational design, and the development of AI tools for city planning.

Versions & Plans

  • Open Source: Free access to research tools and datasets.

How to Use

  • Visit cityform.mit.edu to access tools and resources.
  • Download open-source software and datasets.
  • Apply AI and simulation tools to urban planning projects.

Key Features

  • Urban data analytics and simulation
  • AI-powered planning tools
  • Open-source software and datasets
  • Research-driven innovation
