Woebot

Description

Woebot is an AI-powered mental health chatbot that delivers evidence-based cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and emotional support through conversational interactions. It helps users manage stress, anxiety, and mood with daily check-ins and self-care tools.

Company & History

Woebot Health was founded in 2017 by clinical researchers at Stanford University. The platform is used globally and is backed by clinical studies for digital mental health support.

Key Features

  • 24/7 AI chatbot for mental health support
  • Evidence-based CBT techniques
  • Personalized mood tracking and journaling
  • Educational content and self-care exercises
  • Confidential and stigma-free support

How to Use

  • Download the Woebot app from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Sign up and start chatting with Woebot for daily check-ins.
  • Follow personalized exercises and track your mood over time.

Pricing

  • Free: Core chatbot and self-care features
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for healthcare providers and organizations
