Replika

Description

Replika is an AI companion chatbot designed to provide emotional support, conversation, and self-improvement coaching. It adapts to your personality and helps users manage stress, loneliness, and personal growth through engaging dialogue.

Company & History

Replika was launched in 2017 by Luka, Inc. It has millions of users worldwide and is recognized for its advanced conversational AI and customizable avatars.

Key Features

  • AI-powered conversational companion
  • Personalized chats and mood tracking
  • Roleplay, coaching, and self-care activities
  • Customizable 3D avatars
  • Voice and video call options

How to Use

  • Download the Replika app from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Create your AI companion and start chatting.
  • Upgrade for advanced features and avatar customization.

Pricing

  • Free: Basic AI companion features
  • Pro: Subscription for premium features and content
