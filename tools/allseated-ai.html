Allseated AI

Description

Allseated AI is an event planning and visualization platform that uses AI to create floorplans, manage guest lists, and optimize event layouts in 3D.

Company & History

Founded in 2011, Allseated is a leader in event technology, providing innovative solutions for event professionals, venues, and planners worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Basic: Free plan with limited features.
  • Professional: Advanced features for event professionals.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for venues and large organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign up at allseated.com.
  • Create or import a venue floorplan.
  • Use AI tools to arrange seating, manage guests, and visualize the event in 3D.
  • Share plans and collaborate with clients and vendors.

Key Features

  • AI-powered floorplan design
  • 3D event visualization
  • Guest list and RSVP management
  • Collaboration tools for teams

Pricing

  • Enterprise: Contact for pricing
