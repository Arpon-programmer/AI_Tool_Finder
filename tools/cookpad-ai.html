Cookpad AI

Description

Cookpad AI is an AI-powered cooking assistant that helps chefs and home cooks discover, create, and share recipes based on ingredients, preferences, and trends.

Company & History

Cookpad is a global recipe-sharing platform founded in Japan, leveraging AI to enhance the cooking experience for millions of users worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Access to basic recipe search and sharing.
  • Premium: Advanced features and personalized recommendations.

How to Use

  • Visit cookpad.com.
  • Search for recipes or input ingredients to get suggestions.
  • Save, share, and customize recipes.

Key Features

  • AI-powered recipe suggestions
  • Ingredient-based search
  • Recipe sharing and customization
  • Personalized recommendations

Pricing

  • Free: Basic features
  • Premium: Contact for pricing
