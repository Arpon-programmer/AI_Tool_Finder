Adobe Firefly

Description

Adobe Firefly is a suite of generative AI tools for creative professionals, enabling users to generate images, text effects, and designs from simple prompts. It integrates with Adobe Creative Cloud and is designed for commercial use.

Company & History

Launched by Adobe in 2023, Firefly leverages Adobe's creative expertise and ethical AI practices. It is part of Adobe's ongoing innovation in digital media and design.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited features and credits.
  • Premium: More credits and advanced features.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for organizations.

How to Use

  • Sign in at firefly.adobe.com.
  • Choose a generative tool (image, text effects, etc.).
  • Enter your prompt and customize the output.
  • Download or use results in Adobe apps.

Key Features

  • Text-to-image and text effects generation
  • Integration with Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Commercially safe content
  • Customizable creative controls
  • Multi-language support

Pricing

  • Free: Limited credits
  • Premium: $4.99/month
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
