RunwayML

Description

RunwayML is an AI-powered creative suite for video, image, and audio editing. It offers advanced generative AI tools for video editing, background removal, text-to-video, and real-time collaboration for creators and filmmakers.

Company & History

RunwayML was founded in 2018 and is based in New York. It is used by creative professionals and studios for its cutting-edge AI video and media tools.

Key Features

  • AI video editing and generation
  • Background removal and green screen
  • Text-to-video and image-to-video
  • Real-time collaboration
  • Audio and image editing tools

How to Use

  • Sign up at runwayml.com.
  • Upload your media or start a new project.
  • Use AI tools for editing, generation, and effects.
  • Export your finished video or media files.

Pricing

  • Free plan available
  • Paid plans for advanced features
