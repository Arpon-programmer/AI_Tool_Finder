Sisense AI

Description

Sisense AI brings artificial intelligence and machine learning to the Sisense analytics platform, enabling users to build, embed, and analyze data with advanced AI-driven insights and automation.

Company & History

Sisense, founded in 2004 and based in New York, is a leader in business intelligence and analytics. Sisense AI extends its platform with predictive analytics and embedded AI capabilities.

Key Features

  • AI-powered analytics and dashboards
  • Predictive modeling and forecasting
  • Natural language queries
  • Embedded analytics and white-labeling
  • Integration with data science tools

How to Use

  • Enable Sisense AI features in your Sisense environment.
  • Build dashboards and apply predictive analytics.
  • Use natural language queries for insights.
  • Embed AI analytics into your applications.

Pricing

  • Custom pricing based on business needs
