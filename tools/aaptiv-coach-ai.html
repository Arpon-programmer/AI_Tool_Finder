Aaptiv Coach AI

Description

Aaptiv Coach AI is a digital fitness coach that uses artificial intelligence to deliver personalized audio-based workouts, adaptive training plans, and real-time feedback for a variety of fitness goals.

Company & History

Founded in 2015, Aaptiv is a leading provider of digital fitness solutions, combining AI and expert trainers to help users achieve their health and wellness goals.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited access to workouts and features.
  • Premium: Full access to AI coaching and all workouts (subscription).

How to Use

  • Download the Aaptiv app from aaptiv.com.
  • Set your fitness goals and preferences.
  • Follow AI-generated audio workouts and track your progress.

Key Features

  • AI-powered audio coaching
  • Personalized training plans
  • Real-time feedback and motivation
  • Wide variety of workout types

Pricing

  • Free: Limited features
  • Premium: Subscription required
