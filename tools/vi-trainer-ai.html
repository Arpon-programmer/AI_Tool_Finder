Vi Trainer AI

Description

Vi Trainer AI is a voice-powered fitness coach that uses artificial intelligence to deliver real-time, adaptive audio coaching for running, walking, and fitness training.

Company & History

Developed by Vi Labs, Vi Trainer leverages AI and voice technology to create interactive, personalized fitness experiences for users worldwide.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited access to workouts and features.
  • Premium: Full access to AI coaching and advanced features (subscription).

How to Use

  • Download the Vi Trainer app from vi-labs.com.
  • Set your fitness goals and preferences.
  • Follow real-time AI audio coaching during workouts.

Key Features

  • AI-powered voice coaching
  • Personalized workout plans
  • Real-time feedback and motivation
  • Integration with fitness trackers

Pricing

  • Free: Limited features
  • Premium: Subscription required
