CollectiveAccess AI

Description

CollectiveAccess AI brings artificial intelligence to the open-source CollectiveAccess collections management and cataloguing platform, enabling smarter metadata extraction, image recognition, and automated tagging for museums, archives, and libraries.

Company & History

CollectiveAccess is an open-source project developed by Whirl-i-Gig and a global community. It is widely used by cultural institutions for flexible, standards-based collections management. AI features are being integrated to enhance automation and discovery.

Versions & Plans

  • Open Source: Free, community-supported version.
  • Hosted/Enterprise: Paid hosting, support, and advanced AI features.

How to Use

  • Download or access CollectiveAccess at collectiveaccess.org.
  • Install or sign up for a hosted plan.
  • Enable AI modules for metadata extraction and image recognition.
  • Manage, search, and analyze your collections with AI-powered tools.

Key Features

  • AI-powered metadata extraction
  • Automated image recognition and tagging
  • Flexible cataloguing and search
  • Open-source and highly customizable
  • Integration with external AI services

Pricing

  • Open Source: Free
  • Hosted/Enterprise: Custom pricing
