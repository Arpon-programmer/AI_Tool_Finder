Care Angel

Description

Care Angel is an AI-powered virtual nurse assistant that provides proactive health monitoring, wellness check-ins, and care management for patients and seniors. It uses voice and text to engage, remind, and support individuals at home or in care facilities.

Company & History

Care Angel was founded in 2015 and is trusted by healthcare organizations, payers, and families for its scalable, automated care management solutions.

Key Features

  • AI-driven wellness check-ins and reminders
  • Voice and text-based patient engagement
  • Health monitoring and alerts
  • Integration with care management platforms
  • Personalized support for chronic care and aging in place

How to Use

  • Request a demo or sign up at careangel.com.
  • Set up patient or member profiles for monitoring and reminders.
  • Receive alerts and reports via the Care Angel dashboard or integrations.

Pricing

  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for healthcare organizations and payers
