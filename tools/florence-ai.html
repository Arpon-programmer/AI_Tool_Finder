Florence AI

Description

Florence AI is a virtual nurse assistant that uses AI to help patients manage medications, track health, and get reminders. It provides conversational support and health information for patients and caregivers.

Company & History

Florence was launched in 2017 and is used by healthcare providers and patients worldwide for medication management and health support.

Key Features

  • AI-powered medication reminders
  • Health tracking and daily check-ins
  • Conversational health information
  • Integration with messaging apps (Messenger, WhatsApp, etc.)
  • Personalized support for patients and caregivers

How to Use

  • Access Florence AI via florence.chat or supported messaging apps.
  • Set up medication schedules and health reminders.
  • Chat with Florence for health tips and support.

Pricing

  • Free: Basic features for patients and caregivers
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for healthcare providers
