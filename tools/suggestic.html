Suggestic

Description

Suggestic is an AI-powered platform for personalized nutrition guidance, meal planning, and dietary tracking, designed for individuals and health professionals.

Company & History

Founded in 2014, Suggestic uses AI and data science to deliver tailored nutrition solutions for consumers, coaches, and wellness businesses.

Versions & Plans

  • Personal: Nutrition app for individuals.
  • Professional: Platform for coaches and nutritionists.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for organizations.

How to Use

  • Visit suggestic.com.
  • Set dietary goals and preferences.
  • Receive meal plans, recipes, and nutrition tracking.

Key Features

  • AI-driven personalized nutrition
  • Meal planning and recipe suggestions
  • Dietary tracking and analytics
  • Integration with health apps
