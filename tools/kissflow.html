Kissflow

Description

Kissflow is an AI-enhanced operations and workflow management platform, enabling organizations to automate, track, and optimize business processes with low-code tools and AI insights.

Company & History

Founded in 2012, Kissflow is a global provider of digital workplace and workflow automation solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Key Features

  • AI-powered workflow automation
  • Process design and optimization
  • Integration with business applications
  • Analytics and reporting
  • Low-code/no-code platform

How to Use

  • Request a demo at kissflow.com.
  • Design and automate workflows using the platform.
  • Monitor and optimize business processes with AI insights.
