KNIME AI

Description

KNIME AI is an open-source platform for data analytics, machine learning, and workflow automation, enabling users to build and deploy AI models visually.

Company & History

KNIME was founded in 2004 in Zurich and is widely used by data scientists, analysts, and enterprises for advanced analytics and AI projects.

Versions & Plans

  • Analytics Platform: Free and open-source desktop version.
  • Business Hub: Collaboration, deployment, and enterprise features.
  • Server: Advanced automation, scaling, and integrations.

How to Use

  • Download KNIME Analytics Platform from knime.com.
  • Build workflows using drag-and-drop nodes.
  • Integrate machine learning and AI components.
  • Deploy, share, and automate workflows.

Key Features

  • Visual workflow editor
  • Open-source and extensible
  • Machine learning and AI integrations
  • Enterprise deployment options

Pricing

  • Analytics Platform: Free
  • Business Hub/Server: Contact for pricing
