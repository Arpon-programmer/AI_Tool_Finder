Writesonic

Description

Writesonic is an AI-powered content generation platform for blogs, ads, product descriptions, and more. It helps marketers, writers, and businesses create high-quality written content quickly using advanced language models.

Company & History

Founded in 2020, Writesonic has rapidly grown to serve millions of users worldwide, offering a wide range of AI writing tools and templates.

Versions & Plans

  • Free: Limited words and features.
  • Pro: More words, advanced tools, and priority support.
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large teams.

How to Use

  • Sign up at writesonic.com.
  • Choose a template or tool (blog, ad, etc.).
  • Enter your topic or prompt and generate content.
  • Edit, export, or publish your results.

Key Features

  • AI blog and article writer
  • Ad and product description generator
  • Paraphrasing and summarization tools
  • SEO optimization
  • Integrations and API access

Pricing

  • Free: Limited words
  • Pro: $16/month
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
