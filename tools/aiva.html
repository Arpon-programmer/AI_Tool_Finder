AIVA

Description

AIVA (Artificial Intelligence Virtual Artist) is an AI-powered music composition assistant that helps music producers create original scores and tracks for various genres and purposes.

Company & History

Founded in 2016, AIVA is a pioneer in AI music composition, used by professionals and hobbyists worldwide for film, games, and personal projects.

Key Features

  • AI-generated music in multiple genres
  • Customizable compositions and arrangements
  • MIDI and audio export
  • Collaboration tools
  • Royalty-free licensing

How to Use

  • Sign up at aiva.ai.
  • Choose a style and parameters for your composition.
  • Generate, edit, and export your music.

Pricing

  • Free and paid plans available
