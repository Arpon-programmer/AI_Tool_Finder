Salesforce Einstein

Description

Salesforce Einstein is an integrated set of AI technologies that brings advanced analytics, predictive insights, and automation to the Salesforce CRM platform. It helps sales teams make smarter decisions, automate tasks, and personalize customer interactions.

Company & History

Salesforce Einstein was launched by Salesforce in 2016 as part of its mission to make AI accessible to every business user within the Salesforce ecosystem.

Key Features

  • Predictive lead and opportunity scoring
  • Automated data entry and workflow
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Natural language processing and chatbots
  • Sales forecasting and analytics

How to Use

  • Enable Einstein features in your Salesforce CRM instance.
  • Configure predictive analytics, automation, and AI-powered workflows.
  • Use dashboards and insights to drive sales performance.

Pricing

  • Included with select Salesforce plans
  • Additional costs for advanced features
